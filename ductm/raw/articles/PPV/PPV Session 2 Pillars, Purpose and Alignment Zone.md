## What is the Alignment Zone?

All right, let me just cue up briefly what the alignment zone is. This is where we connect our highest level aspirations. Break it down layer by layer, horizon level by horizon level, meaning different distance ranges out in time down to our daily and hourly actions so that the things we're doing are consistent with our bigger vision for our lives.

So, it's so easy to have these visions and aspirations completely disconnected from what we actually do and spend our time doing day-to-day in our calendar. It's also so easy to spend our days and weeks just reacting to the incoming. The things that people request of us, the obligations, the minutiae, the urgent, but not the important, the things that have deadlines tend to be little reaction things, not the things that will actually move the needle in advance our lives.

So, we tend to do these reactionary things. But by designing this alignment process, we ensure that a good portion of each week is spent doing the things that actually move the needle and advance us towards the things we care most about in life.

---

## Balancing Reactive and Proactive

We also have to do some of the reactionary things. Some of the things that we have obligations day-to-day, week to week, those can't be ignored. But by default, we spend all of our time doing those. Then we never move. We're just running on a treadmill.

So we have to have some time for those as we always do because their urgency dictates it. But we also have to make sure we carve out time each week for the bigger visions, the aspirations, the trajectory we want to advance our life along.

And that by default doesn't happen. That doesn't happen unless you're intentional and careful. And that is what the alignment zone is for is that intentionality, that careful construction, and then protecting a solid portion of your time at least each week, if not every day, but weekly. You should be doing some chunk of things that are advancing you toward your life aspirations as you're doing the day-to-day essential, urgent, often reactionary kinds of things.

So, just wanted to put that in context. That's what we're setting out to accomplish.
