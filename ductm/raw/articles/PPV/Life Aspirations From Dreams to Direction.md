## The Hierarchy: Why and How

One important thing about the whole hierarchy of the system, everything on the layer above is the why. Everything in the layer below is the how.

### Layer by Layer

So for our life aspirations, above is the pillars. The pillars are the why we're doing this aspiration. The layer below is the goals. That's how we're doing the aspiration.

For our goals, the layer above is the life aspirations. That's the why we're doing the goal. The layer below is projects and routines. That is the how we're doing the goal.

For projects, the layer above is the goal. So that's why we're doing the project. The layer below is actions. That's how we're doing the project.

Same thing for routines. Layer above is the goal. That's why. The layer below is the actions. That's how.

It's just consistent layer by layer. Why is the layer above? How is the layer below?

### Finding the Right Connection

So, if you don't know what goal to attach a project to, the question is why are you doing that project? And that will identify the goal.
