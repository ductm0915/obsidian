## Cách Items Đi Vào Remembrance

So how do items go into remembrance? At the high level, two things send items into these collection. The notable button and high value ratings on actions, norbits and cycles. So the notable buttons and high value rating from actions and not cycles feed into these collections.

### Notable Button

So when you click on the notable button, let's go to one of these views for instance. So when you click this, it marks the item as notable. It sends it to your current year's remembrance collection and it creates a bridge between your day-to-day capture and your long-term story and review.

Use notable to capture high signal highlights, wins, major accomplishments, important events, milestones, or meaningful or substantive content. So it could be things like complex piece of work, documented processes, learning or reflection that really move something forward. What matters is that the activity itself mattered to you or that the content mattered to you.

Clicking notable makes sure that the moment doesn't sink to the bottom of the juice box. It goes into remembrance where it can resurface again in long-term reflection. In short, notable means this matters. I want to remember it.

### Value Rating

And next is value. Value is a select rating on actions, neurobits, and cycles that tells you how reusable and contentrich is this page's content. So highv value items from the year automatically surface inside the annual collection. That's why you don't see them here in the property. They're inside the page. We'll get to in a moment where the page itself contains information that you'll want to reuse. For instance, meetings and checklists or drafts or processes. So these pages you want to reference and reuse later.

When you assign high value, you're elevating that page above the rest. And in any other view sorted by value, your best items always rise to the top. So your strongest work is easiest to find.

### Notable vs Value

So side by side, notable focuses on meaning, memory, or substance. Value focuses just on substance and reusability. Together, these two decide which items become part of your long-term story and which item become part of your reusable toolkit. So, this makes sure that your collections are built from things that actually matter to you and the content that actually lasts.
