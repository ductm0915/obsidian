

## Giới Thiệu People Organization

Okay, let's talk about people organization. People organization is a database that organizes your relationship knowledge. This is your relationship knowledge base. This is where you keep the story and context about the relationships themselves.

So, it stores information about individuals or companies and each entry can carry who they are, what they're connected to, and what has happened over time. So each person organization entry is connected to the rest of your system. They're connected to actions. They can connect to GPR entries. They can connect to neurobbits or topics.

So this complements the by people view we've talked about in focus zone. So in focus zone by people is about execution and scheduling what you're doing next related to a person or a company. In knowledge zone, people org is the relationship knowledge base. The context history and links around that relationship and people organization database holds the relationship story and those connections feed into focus zone and gives you the execution lane to act on those relationships with clarity.

## Các Properties Chính

So let's talk about the properties.

### Category

So first we have category right here. We're grouped by these are category based views. The category lets you filter to just work contacts or just personal or connection focused relationships in dashboards and views.

### Status

Status. This shows you how current this relationship is. Active is relationships that are live right now and worth tracking. Inactive means on pause or not currently in motion, but you may come back to them. And archive means this relationship is no longer relevant. You want them out of the active views, but you still keep that history.

### Description

Description. This is a short line of who or what this is. It's especially helpful because it's visible in relational searches. So when you search from actions or from neurobits, that little description line helps you find out who that person is.

So if we open a neurobbit, we come here and we search in people or link existing, you can turn on that little description. And now you can see who this person is and what they do. So again, this very helpful little trick that gives you the preview of just the right information that you need in that view and you can customize it. So that is the description property.

### Next Step

And the next step property, this is a formula that surfaces processor orange prompt with date info. So you can see all of your upcoming action what the next one's date is and also all the future ones that you've scheduled what the dates are or even some of them that have no due dates and it'll also alert you of those type of actions too. So highly highly valuable property.

### Auto Data và Buttons

And auto data. This is a quick summary of status and key relations and the buttons we have new action and new neurobbit. These create new action or new bid that is already linked back to this person or organization.

### Featured In và GPR Relation

Featured in again controls which space dashboard this contact shows up. If you mark someone featured in a space, they can surface in that space regardless of their original category. And GPR relation, this links to the goal, project routine that person or company is involved with.

### Actions và Neurobits

And up the page, we have action. So this relation shows all actions associated with this person organization and neurobid this relation connects to notes and documents that's about this person.

### Topics

So topic this formula shows topics connected to this relationship based on actions and neurobbits that this person is linked to. So you don't need to tag topics directly actually saves you a lot of time. It quietly aggregate topics from your actual actions and neurobbit resources.

And we do have some other properties like located, email, phone, URL. So these are straightforward contact field. You don't have to fill all of these, just the ones that matter to you.

Key dates. You don't need to fill this one if you don't need it. This is where you note important dates connected to this person or organization like the first time you met or started working together, birthdays, anniversaries, any date you want to remember over time.

And then we have some properties here that are autofilled. So you don't need to do anything here. ## Workflow Notes: Quản Lý People

Let's go back to not zone and some workflow notes regarding people management. So for day-to-day use there are two main ways people organization entries get created or link to the process management pipeline.

### Từ GPR

First is from GPR. So when you're in GPR, you can attach an existing person or organization in the side panel. We've talked about this in the GPR uh walkthrough or you can create a new person organization right from that context. So you're really managing the project to people relation directly. So from the high up, you're managing the project to people relation. So you're just going to create a new person or search from existing and tag them.

### Từ Actions và Neurobits

Or you rather connect a person from individual actions and nuris from the lower level units. Sometimes the relationship is more local to a single item. So from actions you can use the people organization field to tach or create a person and from neurobits you can tag a person or organization to show who this knowledge is about.

In both cases you're building out the relationship links as you work without having to leave the page you're on.

## Thói Quen Quan Trọng

A couple of important habits in terms of creating or managing people.

One is you want to sequence GPR design before people. Get your project and routines and goals mapped in GPR first. Then layer people connection on top of that structure instead of trying to manage people in a vacuum.

The second great habit is always search before creating a new person organization so you don't end up with duplicates for the same person or company.

The third good habit is for multiple individuals in the same company. The simplest method is to put the company name in the description. Later you can filter by that keyword in the description and then you can see all the people all the individuals working at that company. So just include that company name in the description and then you can filter by the company. So this is really good for entering individuals in the system and they all work for the same company.
