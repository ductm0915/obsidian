

## Understanding the Two Types of Routines

The term routine is used in two different places in the PPV system.

There are routines in the GPR database, the goals project routines database as part of the alignment zone. The routine entry there is setting your intention and designing the routine to advance a goal. That is a plan. It is an intention. It is an alignment mechanism. That is what we generally just call routines by itself.

Also in PPV, there are routine actions. These are routines that we enter in the action items database in the actions database that are specific instances with a specific due date to do the thing.
