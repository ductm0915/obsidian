# Jeremy Miner

Sales trainer and creator of the **NEPQ (Neuro-Emotional Persuasion Questioning)** methodology. His approach is question-centric — using structured question sequences to bypass buyer resistance by engaging emotions before logic.

## Key Ideas & Contributions
- Created the NEPQ framework as an alternative to traditional pitch-based selling.
- Emphasizes that questions > statements in sales conversations.
- Complementary to [[alex-hormozi]]'s [[aaa-method]] — both prioritize questions and understanding over pushing.
