# Trevor Odum

Trevor Odum is [[caleb-ralston]]'s Content Director and a key figure in building the Ralston personal brand from zero to 265,000 followers in one year.

## Background
- Worked with Caleb for 5+ years before becoming his Content Director.
- Previously led all short-form content for two prominent business creators (billions of views/year, hundreds of shorts/week).
- Progressed from individual contributor (editor) → manager (overseeing editors) → director (overseeing full content operation).
- Was promoted to manager at age 22 after taking ownership of short-form review.

## Key Contributions
- Built an AI database of Caleb's existing content to map talking points and brand pillars before creating new content.
- Set up project management systems (Notion board, Google Drive structure, file naming conventions) from day one.
- Co-produced the flagship 6.5-hour "How to Build a Personal Brand" course — managing 3 cameras, audio, outline scrolling, and post-production as a 2-person team.
- Exemplifies the [[creative-leadership]] philosophy: Caleb delegates ownership → Trevor takes initiative → better output than micromanagement.
