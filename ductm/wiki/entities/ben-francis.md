# Ben Francis

## Overview

Founder of Gymshark, one of the fastest-growing fitness apparel brands globally. Shared branding insights with [[alex-hormozi]] during a day spent together in Las Vegas at the Olympia event.

## Key Contributions to Wiki

- Discussed the **New Balance 70/30 story** with Hormozi: CMO flipped spend from 70% bottom-of-funnel / 30% top-of-funnel to the inverse. First 18 months revenue decreased; month 19 onward, explosive growth.
- Explored with Hormozi how **aspirational branding** works: extreme outcomes get "regressed down" by consumers (Northface survives Everest → keeps me warm walking to my car).
- Along with NL Mack (Gymshark head of brand), helped Hormozi crystallize B2B vs B2C branding strategies.
