# Mitch Harris

"Resident internal AI wizard" at the [[ship-30-for-30]] / [[nicholas-cole]] + [[dicky-bush]] portfolio of writing businesses. Primary instructor for the Claude Co-work Bootcamp 2.0.

---

## Background

- Joined the Ship 30 ecosystem and built AI systems internally for their Premium Ghostwriting Academy
- Automated roughly half of his own workweek using AI, then began doing the same for other companies
- Partners with [[nicholas-cole]] to build and teach the bootcamp
- Self-describes as someone who has "smoke tested" Claude Co-work further than most

---

## Teaching Style

- Highly practical: demos over theory, live building during sessions
- Mantra: **"Tokens in, revenue out"** — if someone pays you for it, have AI help
- Strong on context management as the core skill: "A lot of managing your AI is telling it where to look"
