# Nicholas Cole

Co-founder of [[ship-30-for-30]] and AI Writing School. Runs a portfolio of 8-figure writing businesses alongside Dicky Bush ([[dicky-bush]]). Known as the architect behind the systems; Mitch Harris describes him as "the actual man behind all of the curtain."

---

## Background

- Built a portfolio of writing-focused businesses doing 8 figures in revenue
- Transitioned to integrating AI to scale programs, create efficiencies, and tackle new opportunities
- Co-leads the Claude Co-work Bootcamp alongside AI wizard [[mitch-harris]]
- Launched AI Writing School as part of the [[ship-30-for-30]] ecosystem

---

## Perspective on AI

- Believes AI is a non-optional tool for staying viable in the "new economy"
- References the first one-person $1B company as proof of AI leverage
- Frames skills as "bundles of prompts that run in succession" — enabling you to walk away and return to completed work
- Key insight: skills trigger based on *context*, not manual invocation — "your machine gets smarter" as you build more

---

## Writing Frameworks

- [[prep-the-page]] — Core technique: segment the document into sections before writing to eliminate cognitive overload and writer's block
- [[the-cliff]] — Ending technique: stop after the last main point; no conclusion needed if nothing new to say
