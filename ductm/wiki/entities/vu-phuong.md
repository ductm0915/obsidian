# Cô Vũ Phương

**Organization:** [[wit-organization]]
**Role:** Teacher / Mentor
**Primary Teaching Area:** Công Thức module — community-building formula and karma activation

---

## Overview

Cô Vũ Phương is a teacher at WIT ([[wit-organization]]) who leads the "Công Thức" (Formula) module in the Mentor K07 program. Her teaching name "Phương" is how she refers to herself in sessions ("Phương muốn giao lưu với cả nhà...").

She teaches the foundational framework for how WIT Mentors build expert communities ([[cong-thuc-cong-dong-chuyen-gia]]) and the deeper metaphysical mechanism of activating tổng nghiệp như ý ([[tong-nghiep-nhu-y]]) — total karmic alignment.

---

## Teaching Style

Cô Vũ Phương's style is conversational and Socratic. She consistently asks the class to arrive at answers themselves before offering her framing. She uses "Phương muốn giao lưu cùng với cả nhà" (Phương wants to exchange with everyone) rather than "I want to teach" — reflecting WIT's culture of mutual learning rather than one-directional instruction.

She is notably transparent about her own position on the path: "Phương cũng đang trên hành trình để hướng đến công thức này thôi" (Phương is also still on the journey toward this formula) — modeling the WIT value of liêm chính (integrity/transparency).

She frequently draws from her experience as both observer and participant in WIT's community-building practices, pointing to examples from [[hong-trieu-bao]] and senior teachers as living demonstrations of the formula.

---

## Primary Module: Công Thức (Formula)

Cô Vũ Phương teaches the Công Thức module across 7 sessions in Mentor K07 (January 2026):
- Sessions 1–5: Công Thức Tạo Cộng Đồng Chuyên Gia (Expert Community Formula)
- Sessions 6–7: Công Thức Kích Hoạt Tổng Nghiệp Như Ý (Karma Activation Formula)

Key concepts she introduces:
- The distinction between đồng ngôn, đồng thuận, đồng hành (three levels of alignment)
- The Lãnh đạo + Tả Phù + Hữu Bật triad (leader + two supporters) as the minimum community unit
- NHÂN TỐT + DUYÊN LÀNH = QUẢ NHƯ Ý as the foundational karma formula
- Quả của thức / quả của duyên / quả của quả (three dimensions of desired outcomes)

## Context Within WIT

Cô Vũ Phương is a Hậu Master or Master-level practitioner — she mentions being trained by [[hong-trieu-bao]] and having observed him apply the community formula over multiple Mentor cohorts. She is one of the teachers in WIT's senior educator cadre, alongside [[que-minh]] and Cô Hiến (who facilitates sessions) among others.

She references "các thầy cô trong ban tổ chức và ban phụng sự" (teachers in the organizing and service committees) as the broader community of WIT educators she is part of.
