# Founder OS

Matt Gray's paid community and program for founders building profitable personal brands. Described as "my life's work."

---

## Scale & Stats

- 225,000+ newsletter subscribers
- 13,000+ founders helped
- Trustpilot: 90+ five-star reviews
- LinkedIn drives ~50% of leads; newsletter drives ~60% of profits

## What It Offers

- 180+ systems for building and scaling a founder brand
- Custom AI agent trained on 3M+ words of Matt Gray's public and private systems
- 50+ live sessions every month (Q&As, workshops)
- Epic founder retreats hosted annually (Bali, Sedona, Mexico City, Dolomites)
- Community of "high-growth founders" building founder-led brands

## Purpose

A productized implementation of the [[founder-flywheel]], [[content-gps]], and [[aed-system]] — packaged into a structured community experience for founders who want accountability, peers, and ready-made systems.

## Offer Stack

Uses the [[offer-stack-model]] with 3 tiers:
- **Entry Level** (Personal BrandOS, YouTube for CEOs, LinkedIn for CEOs) — $29–$300; launched ~quarterly; primarily lead generation
- **Program** — $7.8K; 30 founders/month; access to team + systems + 1 session/month with Matt
- **Velocity** — $36K; 15 founders/month; Slack community + weekly access to Matt + 50+ live sessions
- **Edge** — $100K–$250K; 3 founders/month; weekly 1:1s with Matt + C-suite, text access, in-person sessions

Uses [[application-router]] to route applicants to the right tier based on business stage.
