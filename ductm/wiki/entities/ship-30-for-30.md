# Ship 30 For 30

Writing program and business ecosystem founded by [[nicholas-cole]] and [[dicky-bush]]. Centers around writing as a skill set, and increasingly uses AI to accelerate and scale their programs.

---

## Programs

- **Ship 30 For 30** — Original writing cohort program
- **AI Writing School** — AI-focused writing acceleration program
- **Claude Co-work Bootcamp** — Hands-on AI tooling bootcamp (led by [[mitch-harris]])
- **Operators Accelerator** — Higher-tier program running concurrently with bootcamp

---

## Business Model

- Portfolio of writing businesses doing 8 figures in revenue (Cole + Bush)
- Open-sourced their "resident AI expert" ([[mitch-harris]]) to their community as a competitive moat
- Uses AI to multiply the leverage of the existing writing skill set

---

## AI Approach

> *"We use it to run a business. We're not really AI influencers."* — [[nicholas-cole]]

AI is applied practically to generate leverage within existing business operations, not adopted for novelty.
