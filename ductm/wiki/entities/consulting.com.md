# Consulting.com

**Founder:** [[sam-ovens]]
**Type:** Online education platform → pivoted to [[skool-platform]]
**Status:** Historical — predecessor to Skool

## Overview

Consulting.com was [[sam-ovens]]'s primary business before Skool — an online education company that taught people how to start and scale consulting businesses. It was one of the most successful online education businesses of its era.

The transition from Consulting.com to Skool represented Sam's shift from being in the **content/education business** to the **software/platform business** — a decision he has discussed at length as an application of [[long-term-thinking]] and [[second-order-consequences]].

## Significance in the Wiki

Referenced primarily in the context of Sam's entrepreneurial journey and his insights about:
- The limitations of the info-product business model (see [[info-vs-software]])
- Why he chose to build Skool instead of scaling Consulting.com further
- The community design principles he learned from running Consulting.com's community
