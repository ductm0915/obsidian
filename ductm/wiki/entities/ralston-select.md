# Ralston Select

Rson Select (also referred to as Rston Consulting) is [[caleb-ralston]]'s consulting firm focused on helping entrepreneurs and brands scale through personal branding, content strategy, and media team optimization.

## Key Details
- High-ticket consulting offering — clients come through private channels (DMs, texts, emails) rather than public comments.
- Received 750+ applications in the first 10 months of Caleb's personal brand launch.
- Content serves dual purpose: (1) grow business, (2) educate people for free who will never become clients.
- Case study: helped a client go from 4-5K leads/video to 20K leads/video by shifting from entertainment to educational content.
