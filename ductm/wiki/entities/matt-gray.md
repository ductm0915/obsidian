# Matt Gray

Entrepreneur, founder-brand builder, and creator of the **Founder OS** system. Built a personal brand audience of 3.2M+ followers across platforms (16M+ including business brands) with 81M organic impressions/month — without spending money on ads.

---

## Background

- Began his career building company brands; shifted to personal brand around age 30
- Pivoted from entrepreneur behind the scenes → "portfolio manager" mindset running multiple businesses under a personal brand umbrella
- Grew X following to 40K+ in months → LinkedIn to 840K+ → expanded to Instagram, YouTube, TikTok
- Built 4 profitable businesses over 15 years

## Core Philosophy

- **Media-first founder**: Personal brand is the primary business asset — it survives company exits and compounds over time
- **Insane generosity**: Core personal value; give everything freely, monetize through trust
- **Long-term compounding**: Brand-building is a 5–10 year asset, not a 6-month growth hack
- **Entrepreneurship as soulful journey**: Entrepreneurship = "the most epic meditation you could ever go on"
