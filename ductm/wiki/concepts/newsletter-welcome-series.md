# Newsletter Welcome Series

[[matt-gray]]'s 9-email automated sequence that converts new newsletter subscribers into paying customers. Runs automatically for every subscriber in the first 9 days; Matt credits this with generating millions of dollars.

---

## The Two-Phase Structure

### Phase 1: Educational Series (5 emails)

| Email | Content |
|---|---|
| #1 — Immediate Value | Deliver the lead magnet promised at signup |
| #2 — Origin Story | How you got to doing what you do; hero's journey; why you're doing this |
| #3 — Social Proof | Highlight someone you've helped; a success story |
| #4 — Set Expectations | What to expect as part of the community/newsletter going forward |
| #5 — Soft Introduction | Gentle introduction to your paid offering (no hard sell) |

### Phase 2: FOMO Series (4 emails)

More direct sales; typically 4-6 emails. Contents:
- Success stories / testimonials from customers
- Benefits of your product; big problems solved
- Clear articulation of what you do and how you do it
- Clear CTA: sales call (for communities/courses/agencies) or purchase link (for SaaS/e-commerce)

## Newsletter Mechanics

**Signup page:** Focused; no distractions. Components: bold value proposition, credibility (testimonials), clear promise, one core email cadence statement.

**Lead Magnet:** Offer something "so insanely generous that people can't help but sign up." Options: checklist, book chapter, resource, diagram, system, playbook, blueprint.

**Subject Lines:** The most important element (like packaging for YouTube, hook for a thread). Types:
- Curiosity: "This mistake cost me $100,000"
- Benefit: "How to 10x your content reach"
- Personal: "My biggest failure and what it taught me"
- Number-driven: "7 systems that built my $13M business"

---

## How It Fits the Revenue System

The welcome series is Bridge #2 in Matt Gray's 3-bridge monetization system:
1. Strategic CTAs (Bridge 1) → drive people to newsletter
2. **Newsletter Welcome Series (Bridge 2)** → build trust + introduce offer
3. Discovery Call Framework (Bridge 3) → close the sale

Newsletter subscribers are worth far more than social followers: **120% better conversion rate** due to permission-based relationship. See [[rented-vs-owned-audience]].
