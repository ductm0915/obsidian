# Phase Shift Metaphor

## The Metaphor

Sourced by [[alex-hormozi]] from Brian Johnson (Blueprint longevity):

> "You've been training your entire life to swim. You go from a lap pool → a lake → the open ocean → free swimming border-to-border. You get better and better as a swimmer.
>
> But the change that's about to happen is a phase shift. The water boils, evaporates, and now you're flapping your arms in gas. It doesn't matter how good of a swimmer you are — the fundamental physics of the environment have changed."

---

## Why This Matters

Most mental models for "disruption" assume *continuous change* — harder waves, colder water, farther distances. Swimmers can train for those. A phase shift is categorically different: the *medium* itself changes state.

Prior skill mastery becomes irrelevant — not because you got worse, but because the skill set no longer operates in the same physical environment.

---

## Applications

| Old framing | Phase-shift framing |
|---|---|
| "AI is a better tool for my existing job" | "The job-as-concept is becoming a different state of matter" |
| "I just need to learn some AI skills" | "The environment I was trained in no longer exists" |
| "My 20 years of experience has value" | "Experience in the old medium doesn't transfer to the new one" |

---

## The Useful Response

A phase shift is not cause for despair — it's cause for reorientation:
- Humans are slow to adapt. This means the *transition window* (where the new environment is already here but most haven't adapted yet) is the maximum opportunity window.
- Over-50s who accumulated wealth in the old medium will be slow to adapt → wealth migrates to those who adapted.
- Price sensitivity is also slow to adapt → arbitrage opportunity exists in the spread between new cost basis and old pricing.

## Relationship to Barbell Strategy

The phase shift is the *why* behind the [[barbell-strategy-ai]]:
- Left bar (AI-native) = swimming in gas from day one.
- Right bar (timeless bets) = things that exist regardless of the medium (bodies, food, shelter, entertainment).
