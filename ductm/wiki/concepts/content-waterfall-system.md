# Content Waterfall System

[[matt-gray]]'s system for repurposing one pillar piece of content into 36 derivative pieces across all platforms — enabling an omnipresent content strategy without proportionally increasing creative effort.

---

## The Cascade

Starting from **1 long-form YouTube video** (~12 min = ~2,500 words):

```
1 YouTube Video
├── 3 Instagram Reels (best 60-second clips)
│   ├── 3 YouTube Shorts (same clips)
│   └── 3 TikTok Videos (same clips)
├── 10 X Tweets (copy nuggets from the transcript)
├── 1 Newsletter (published 6 weeks later)
└── 3 LinkedIn Long-Form Posts (copy sections)

Total: ~21 pieces (+ more with further derivatives)
```

Matt claims the system produces 36+ pieces from a single source.

## Why It Works

- A 12-minute video contains ~2,500 words of copy — enough for multiple written formats
- Different platforms need different format lengths but often the same ideas
- Enables **delegation**: once the pillar piece is created, team members handle the derivatives
- Prevents content hamster wheel: creator focuses on one great piece/week; team handles distribution

## Platform-Specific Notes

| Destination | Format | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram Reels | 60-sec clips | Long-form video |
| YouTube Shorts | Same clips | Long-form video |
| TikTok | Same clips | Long-form video |
| X (Twitter) | Atomic tweets + threads | Video transcript copy |
| Newsletter | Full essay | Video transcript |
| LinkedIn | Long-form posts | Video transcript |

## Relationship to Other Frameworks

- Operationalizes [[accordion-method]]: high-volume derivatives from a single optimized source
- Part of [[content-gps]] "Content Multiplication" layer
- Supports the [[founder-flywheel]]: one great piece fuels the entire flywheel

## Delegation Application

When using the waterfall system with a team:
- Creator makes 1 great piece/week
- Social Media Manager distributes derivatives
- Writer packages copy for newsletters/LinkedIn/X
- Video Editor cuts short-form from long-form

See [[aed-system]] for the time-management framework to support this.
