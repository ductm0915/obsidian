# AED System

**Automate → Eliminate → Delegate.** [[matt-gray]]'s framework for auditing how a founder spends time and systematically removing low-leverage work from their plate. Time is the scarcest resource; focus is the most valuable.

---

## The Process

### Step 1: Time Audit
List every task completed over the past month in a Google Sheet. For each task, assign one column: Automate, Eliminate, or Delegate.

### Step 2: Eliminate First
If a task shouldn't be done at all, don't delegate or automate it — just remove it. No sense in delegating what shouldn't exist.

### Step 3: Automate
For remaining tasks: can a tool handle this? (HypeFury, HubSpot, Zapier, ChatGPT, etc.) Automate so it runs on autopilot without recurring founder involvement.

### Step 4: Delegate
For tasks that can't be automated: delegate with documentation.
- Film a Loom video explaining the task
- Assign a specific owner
- Make them the **systems designer** going forward — they own it, improve it, and report on it

---

## Priority Order

> Eliminate → Automate → Delegate

Always eliminate first. Delegating or automating something that shouldn't exist is waste compounded.

---

## Delegation Math

To decide whether to hire, calculate the ROI:

```
ROI = (Hourly Rate × Hours Saved per Month) / Monthly Hire Cost
```

**Example from Matt:**
- Hourly rate: $6,000/hour
- Social media manager cost: $8,000/month
- Hours saved: 80 hours/month
- ROI: ($6,000 × 80) / $8,000 = **60x return**

The freed hours must be reallocated to higher-leverage revenue-generating activities — otherwise the math doesn't hold.

## Personal Media Company Roles

The AED System leads naturally to building a "personal media company" around the founder:

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Social Media Manager | Distributes all content across platforms |
| Writer | Packages ideas → X threads, LinkedIn posts, newsletters |
| Video Editor | Long-form cuts + short-form clips |
| Tech Operator | Landing pages, CRM, automation piping |
| Chief of Staff | Calendar optimization, internal comms, removes distractions |
| Closer | Runs sales calls; target 30%+ close rate |
