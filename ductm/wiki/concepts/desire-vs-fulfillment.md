# Desire vs. Fulfillment

The gap between **desire** (the wanting) and **fulfillment** (the having) is structurally asymmetric: desire is anticipatory and motivating; fulfillment is temporary and immediately replaced by new desire.

## Two Frameworks

### Vipassana Framework
- Desire (tanha) is itself the suffering, not the absence of its object
- Fulfillment provides brief pleasure, then desire reasserts
- The solution: observe the sensation of desire arise and pass — it is impermanent like all sensations
- See: [[hedonic-adaptation]], [[four-noble-truths]]

### Hormozi / Marketing Framework
- The desire state is where customers buy (point of deprivation)
- Fulfillment temporarily removes the motivation to buy
- Business insight: sell at the moment of maximum desire, not after satisfaction
- See: [[sell-at-point-of-deprivation]], [[deprivation-motivation]]
