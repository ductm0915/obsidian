# Content GPS

[[matt-gray]]'s master content system, organized into three layers: **Foundation** (what to create), **Multiplication** (how to scale it), and **Distribution** (how to reach people). The GPS eliminates "what do I post?" forever.

---

## Layer 1: Content Foundation

### 4 Content Categories
Define 4 recurring themes that form the "drumbeat" of your marketing strategy. Matt's example: personal branding, personal stories, audience growth systems, founder lifestyle design.

These categories are not rigid — flexibility and fun matter. But they give content a thematic backbone that creates association.

### Ideal Customer Research
Before creating, survey 10-20 dream customers via Typeform:
- "What is your biggest challenge right now?"
- "What is your biggest goal by end of year?"
- "What's keeping you up at night?"

Upload results to an LLM → generate 100 content topics aligned to real pain points.

### Two Creator Types
- **Expert**: Already an expert — teach from experience
- **Curator**: Building expertise — document the journey, share lessons, books, mentors

Both are valid. Choose based on where you currently stand.

### Content IP
Branded terms and phrases that link back to the creator:
- Examples: "Extreme Ownership" (Jocko Willink), "Deep Work" (Cal Newport), "10,000 Hours" (Gladwell)
- Matt's examples: "Personal Moat," "Content Waterfall System," "The Four W's"
- Over time, people use your phrases in normal conversation → automatic brand association

### Content Batching
Film/write in batches to avoid constant creation pressure. Matt films ~4 YouTube videos over 5 days → 4-6 weeks of content. Batching separates the **CEO role** (building systems) from the **creator role** (making content).

---

## Layer 2: Content Multiplication

The [[content-waterfall-system]]: transform 1 pillar piece into 36 derivative pieces across all platforms.

**Core mechanic:** A 12-minute YouTube video ≈ 2,500 words = 10 tweets + 1 newsletter + 3 LinkedIn posts + 3 IG reels + 3 YT shorts + 3 TikToks.

---

## Layer 3: Content Distribution

Platform-specific tactics:

| Platform | Cadence | Key Tactic |
|---|---|---|
| YouTube | 1 high-quality video/month minimum | Quality > quantity; 80% battle = packaging (title + thumbnail + idea) |
| X | 2 posts/day (8am + 2pm EST) + 4 long-form/week | Use HypeFury for scheduling; all CTAs → newsletter |
| LinkedIn | Daily posts (no scheduling tools) | Always include image; CTA → newsletter |
| Instagram | 1 carousel + 1 reel/day | 8am + 1pm; use Figma for grid aesthetics |
| TikTok | Repurpose IG reels | No additional creation needed |
| Newsletter | 2x/week | Primary owned audience channel |

**Metrics Philosophy:**
- Don't obsess over vanity metrics (algorithm goes up and down)
- 1-2 metrics per platform max
- Monthly audit: identify top 10 and bottom 10 performers → double down on winning patterns

## Full System Summary

```
Ideal Customer Research
         ↓
4 Content Categories + Content IP
         ↓
Create Pillar Piece (batch)
         ↓
Content Waterfall System (→ 36 pieces)
         ↓
Platform-Specific Distribution
         ↓
Monthly Performance Audit → Refine
```
