# 7 Sự Giàu Toàn Diện (7 Comprehensive Richnesses)

**Origin:** [[wit-organization]], taught by [[hong-trieu-bao]]
**Core function:** The ultimate destination and measuring standard for all WIT programs

---

## Summary

"7 Sự Giàu Toàn Diện" is WIT's holistic wealth framework. It redefines "richness" to include seven dimensions of human flourishing, in contrast to the conventional view that only material wealth (#7) counts. The seven richnesses are presented as the natural endpoint of complete personal development (nhận thức đủ đầy — see [[nhan-thuc-du-day]]).

The framework appears verbatim in the lời biết ơn (gratitude preface) of every THNK K36 session, indicating its foundational status across all WIT programs.

## How the Framework Works

The ordering is intentional: richnesses #1 through #6 are the inner foundation; #7 (material) is the natural fruit. WIT's core claim is that pursuing material wealth without the inner richnesses leads to instability — money without wisdom, without character, without health, is not true richness.

Conversely, developing the inner richnesses in sequence naturally produces material abundance without the brittleness that comes from pursuing money alone.

The three energy states that characterize someone living the 7 richnesses: **an vui** (joyfulness), **bao dung** (magnanimity), **trân trọng – biết ơn** (appreciation-gratitude). These three states appear everywhere in WIT teaching as both the means and the mark of development.

### The Legacy Dimension

The framework includes an implicit generational dimension. A person who achieves the 7 richnesses becomes a "legacy builder" (người xây dựng di sản). The teaching: "Khi một người ngộ ra, cả gia tộc sẽ thay đổi." (When one person fully realizes this, the entire family lineage changes.) This frames personal development as not merely self-improvement but as changing inherited patterns across generations.

---

## Contrast with Conventional Views

The teacher opens Session 1 of THNK K36 by identifying 4 "vấn nạn" (crises) in modern life. His argument is that modern people try to solve each crisis (relationship crisis, financial crisis, health crisis, meaning crisis) in isolation — which doesn't work. The solution is to orient toward all 7 richnesses simultaneously, since they are interlocked.

**Common error:** Treating #7 (Giàu Vật Chất) as the goal and the other six as nice-to-haves. WIT reverses this: the other six are the path; #7 is the outcome.

## Connection to Other Frameworks

- The 7 richnesses map onto the 4 life domains: Giàu Nhân Cách / Giàu Phẩm Chất / Giàu Trí Tuệ / Giàu Tâm Thái (inner self domain); Giàu Thể Chất (health domain); richnesses 1–5 (relationship domain); Giàu Vật Chất (finance domain).
- The [[15-khai-niem-nguon]] are the tools to achieve the 7 richnesses within each of the 4 domains.
- The starting point for achieving any of the 7 richnesses is [[nhan-thuc-du-day]] — sufficient awareness — about that domain.
- The causal chain [[tam-thuc-tu-duy-hanh-vi]] explains why inner richnesses precede outer: consciousness must be right before thinking can be right, before actions can be right, before results can be right.
