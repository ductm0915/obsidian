# Habit Pattern

A **habit pattern** is an automatic stimulus-response loop — behavior that has been repeated enough times to become unconscious and self-sustaining.

## Vipassana Interpretation

Goenka maps habit patterns directly to **sankara** (mental conditionings). Every time the mind reacts to a sensation with craving or aversion, it deepens the corresponding sankara/habit. Vipassana breaks habit patterns by inserting **equanimous observation** between stimulus and response.
