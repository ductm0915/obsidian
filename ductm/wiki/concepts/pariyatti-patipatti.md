# Pariyatti & Paṭipatti — Study and Practice

Pariyatti (theoretical study/learning of the Dhamma texts) and paṭipatti (actual experiential practice of meditation) are the two essential halves of the Dhamma. Goenka's emphasis: paṭipatti (practice) is always the primary aim; pariyatti must serve practice, not replace it. A third term, pativedha (realization/penetration), is the fruit. The danger is mistaking intellectual understanding for the actual liberation. The "gem set in gold" metaphor: practice is the gold setting that gives the gem of study its value.
