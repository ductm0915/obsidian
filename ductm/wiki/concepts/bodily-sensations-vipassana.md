# Bodily Sensations in Vipassana

**Bodily sensations** (vedanā) are the primary objects of observation in Vipassana meditation. Every experience — thought, emotion, memory — has a corresponding physical manifestation as sensation in the body.

This is the key discovery Goenka attributes to the Buddha: the "missing link" in the chain from sense contact to craving is **sensation**. Previous teachers jumped from contact → craving, missing the intermediate vedanā step.
