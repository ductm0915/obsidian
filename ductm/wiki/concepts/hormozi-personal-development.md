# Hormozi — Personal Development Framework

Hormozi's approach to personal development rejects conventional self-help in favor of a results-first, character-through-action model.

## Core Thesis

> "You don't think your way to being a better person. You do your way to being a better person. Doing is being." — Hormozi, How to Win

Character is not something you develop through introspection — it emerges through consistent high-standard action. This reverses the conventional mindset → action sequence.

## Key Frameworks

### My Fault Philosophy
Everything that happens in your life is your responsibility to respond to. Not blame, but ownership. Source: [[my-fault-philosophy]]

### The Anger as Fuel Model
Channeling frustration into productive work rather than suppressing or venting. Source: [[hormozi-best-year-2026]]

### Cringe-Status Play
Willingness to document failure and struggle publicly — no ego protection → faster learning → faster growth. Source: [[cringe-status-play]]

### Volume Negates Luck
Increase output volume to bypass luck/timing as a variable. Source: [[volume-negates-luck]]
