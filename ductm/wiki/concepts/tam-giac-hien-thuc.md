# Tam Giác Hiện Thực (Reality Triangle)

WIT's foundational framework for understanding, receiving, transferring, and creating any reality. Taught in the 3 Hệ Quy Chiếu Chuẩn module (see [[wit-k07-he-quy-chieu]]). The Reality Triangle asserts that every manifestation — physical objects, emotions, relationships, financial states — consists of three components in dynamic interrelation.

## The Core Structure

```
        THÔNG TIN
       (Information)
           /\
          /  \
         /    \
 NĂNG LƯỢNG —— VẬT CHẤT
  (Energy)   (Matter/Form)
```

**Thông tin** (Information): All conceptual content — beliefs, worldviews, knowledge, mental models, khái niệm nguồn (source concepts). The "know" and "believe" layers of reality.

**Năng lượng** (Energy): Emotional force, relational vitality, motivation, the living charge of reality. The feeling-state and relational field.

**Vật chất** (Matter/Form): Physical, tangible manifestation. The observable, measurable result. The "what can be pointed to."

Any situation, any goal, any problem can be mapped onto this triangle: what is the information layer (what beliefs/concepts are generating it), what is the energy layer (what emotional or relational forces sustain it), and what is the matter layer (what physical reality is produced).

## Operating Principles

**1. The triangle is generative, not descriptive.** It doesn't just describe reality — it creates it. When you change the information layer, the energy and matter layers shift accordingly. "Khi mình đổi được cái góc nhìn thì cái biết, tin, hiểu của mình nó đi đúng hướng. Và cuộc sống của mình nó lập tức nó sẽ đổi chiều." (When you shift your reference frame, your knowledge-belief-understanding aligns and life immediately changes.)

**2. The triangle is fluid, not rigid.** "Đừng có cố gắng vật chất hóa nó. Khi nó thành vật chất nó sẽ cô đặc." (Don't crystalize it. When it becomes purely material it stiffens.) The framework is a generative lens, not a formula to apply mechanically.

**3. Reality can be transferred.** Being in proximity to someone with a positive reality configuration allows transfer of their energy-information pattern: "Mình chỉ cần ở gần người có hạnh phúc thì năng lượng của mình cũng chuyển sang." (Simply being near a happy person begins to shift your energy.)

**4. The triangle nests infinitely.** Any grouping of three related concepts can be mapped as a triangle. Hundreds of nested triplets can be built within the core structure. Examples:

| Triplet | Domain |
|---|---|
| Biết / Tin / Hiểu | Epistemology (Know / Believe / Understand) |
| Nhân / Duyên / Quả | Causality (Cause / Condition / Effect) |
| Đạo lý / Tôn giáo / Khoa học | Reference systems (Moral law / Religion / Science) |
| Tâm / Thân / Nghiệp | Health (Mind / Body / Karma) |
| Tâm / Tánh / Tình | Inner architecture (Mind / Nature / Feeling) |
| Why / How / What | Action framework (Purpose / Method / Output) |
| Nhân sinh quan / Vũ trụ quan / Thế giới quan | Worldview (Human / Cosmic / Societal) |
| Khái niệm hóa / Trạng thái hóa / Hình ảnh hóa | Practice (Conceptualize / Embody / Visualize) |

## Three Standard Reference Systems

The Reality Triangle intersects with three distinct epistemological frameworks (hệ quy chiếu) through which people evaluate any situation:

- **Khoa học** (Science): empirical, analytical, proof-requiring
- **Đạo lý** (Moral Law): principle-based, trust-action-result
- **Tôn giáo / Tín ngưỡng** (Religion / Spiritual Belief): karma, merit, nhân duyên quả

These three form their own triangle. A person's dominant hệ quy chiếu shapes which information layer drives their reality. Most people unconsciously operate from one reference system while believing they operate from another.

## Applications by Domain

**Health triangle**: Tâm (mental/emotional health) / Thân (physical health) / Nghiệp (karmic/habitual patterns). "Sức khỏe không đơn giản chỉ là thể chất — nó là toàn diện tâm-thân-nghiệp."

**Financial reality**: Must begin with the appropriate triangle — typically through the lens of merit (công đức phước đức) and connection (duyên), before moving to clear mental models (thông tin) and energetic alignment.

**Relational reality**: The Why-How-What triangle maps organizational and family roles. A person occupying the Why position (vision-holder) at work must often shift to How or What at home, depending on context.

**Practice**: "Nhận, chuyển và tạo hiện thực cho chính mình cũng như cho người khác." (Receive, transfer, and create reality — for yourself and others.) These three operations are the practitioner's core skills.

## Connection to Coaching Practice

In WIT's Mentor program, the Reality Triangle is the fundamental tool for:
1. **Diagnosing** a client's current reality (which layer is misaligned?)
2. **Transferring** positive reality patterns (exposing client to the right energy-information field)
3. **Creating** new realities (working at the information layer to shift the energy and eventually matter layers)

The framework is taught as accessible: "Cái khác biệt nó không nằm ở cái hoàn cảnh mà nằm ở hiện thực. Và cái hiện thực được tạo bởi thông tin, năng lượng và vật chất." (The difference between people doesn't lie in their circumstances but in their reality — and reality is made of information, energy, and matter.)
