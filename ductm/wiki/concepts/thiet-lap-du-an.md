# Thiết Lập Dự Án (Project Setup)

## Core Definition

> **Thiết lập dự án là quá trình khái niệm hóa, hình ảnh hóa và trạng thái hóa điều mà mình muốn làm, để xây dựng niềm tin bản thân và tìm kiếm sự đồng thuận của con người.**

Project setup is the process of conceptualizing (khái niệm hóa), visualizing (hình ảnh hóa), and state-calibrating (trạng thái hóa) what one wants to do, in order to build self-trust (niềm tin bản thân) and seek the consensus of people (đồng thuận của con người).

This definition comes from WIT's PD course "Nâng Tầm Thiết Lập Dự Án" taught by [[hong-trieu-bao]]. See [[wit-pd-thiet-lap-du-an]] for full course documentation.

## What Makes This Distinct from Conventional Project Management

Conventional project management begins with external planning: scope definition, timelines, resource allocation, risk assessment. It assumes the project's rationale is already settled and the task is coordination.

WIT's approach begins one level earlier: with the *inner coherence* of the project's creator. The claim is that most projects fail not due to poor planning but due to **inner incoherence** — the creator does not fully believe in the project, does not have a clear image of it, or carries ambivalent inner states when discussing it. These inner gaps communicate to potential collaborators and investors through subtle channels, eroding the consensus-building needed for the project to manifest.

| Conventional PM | WIT Thiết Lập Dự Án |
|---|---|
| Starts from scope and deliverables | Starts from the creator's inner state |
| Seeks resource alignment | Seeks self-alignment first, then others' alignment |
| Success metric: on time, on budget | Success metric: genuine consensus earned |
| Tool: Gantt chart, risk matrix | Tool: the three-hóa process |
| Assumes the "why" is clear | Explicitly works to clarify the "why" at the consciousness level |

## The Three-Hóa Process (Ba Quá Trình Hóa)

"Hóa" means to transform, convert, or crystallize. The three processes each operate on three dimensions:

### Hình Ảnh Hóa (Image Crystallization)

The mental image of the project must become *stable and self-generating* — reproducible from memory without reference to documents, consistent across different conversational contexts, detailed enough to transmit to others. If closing your project notes makes the image fade, it has not been fully image-crystallized. The standard: you can describe the project in any setting and produce the same clear picture.

### Khái Niệm Hóa (Concept Crystallization)

Every element of the project must be made explicit as a precise concept. This is not just naming things — it is being able to define, explain, and contextualize each component so that a listener who doesn't share your assumptions can understand it. Vague concepts like "I want to help people" must be sharpened into specific, testable understandings.

### Trạng Thái Hóa (State Crystallization)

When you think about, discuss, or present your project, your inner state must be *balanced and positively oriented (cân bằng, hướng dương)*. A project that makes you feel anxious, uncertain, or defensive when described to others has not been state-crystallized. The state the creator carries in the moment of communication is transmitted alongside the content — people sense incongruence even if they cannot name it.

These three processes are applied recursively: to image-crystallize, you must also concept-crystallize and state-crystallize the image itself; and so on for each of the three dimensions.

## The Logic Chain: From Consciousness to Consensus

The causal chain runs:

```
Consciousness clarity → Self-trust → Self-consensus → 
Inner conviction → Communication with others → 
Their trust → Their consensus → Project manifests
```

No step can be skipped. Attempting to persuade others before achieving self-consensus produces hollow promotion — technically adequate but energetically empty.

## The 7-Step Project Framework

From Session B04 of the PD course:

1. **Động lực và Động cơ (Motivation and Drive):** Why this, why now, what inner pull sustains this through difficulty?
2. **Đối tượng (Target Person):** Who specifically is this for? Define a real human being, not an abstraction.
3. **Mong muốn (Desires):** Both the creator's deepest desires and the target person's genuine desires — identify the overlap.
4. **Mục tiêu (Goals):** Specific targets that emerge naturally from clarified desires.
5. **Cách thức thực hiện (Implementation Method):** Domain-specific operational details — curriculum design for education projects, sales strategy for business projects, etc.
6. **Thời gian (Timeline):** Realistic phases with adequate time allocated to each.
7. **Kết quả kỳ vọng (Expected Results):** Success expressed not just as numbers but as images and states — what will it feel like when this works?

**Important:** The 7 steps provide structure, but the *soul* of the project must come from the creator's consciousness. A document that passes through the 7 steps mechanically but without genuine inner alignment is still fragile.

## Alignment with the "Triangle of Law" (Tam Giác Giới)

From B05: for a project to be viable in society, it must align with all three layers of social authority:

- **Chính phủ (Government/Law):** Legal compliance; the project must operate within current regulations
- **Người giàu / Tổ chức (Wealthy/Institutions):** Endorsement or at least non-opposition from those who hold resources and legitimacy
- **Người dân (People/Society):** Genuine service to and alignment with ordinary people's real needs

A project strong on only one or two layers remains exposed. True durability requires thuận tam giác giới — harmonizing with all three.

## Project Setup as Money Attraction

From B01: project setup is one of three methods of attracting money, specifically the method that enriches money's *information/knowledge dimension* (thông tin):

- **Thiết lập dự án** → enriches Tri thức (knowledge/information) → attracts money's information form
- **Tập hợp con người** (assembling people) → enriches Năng lượng (energy/human capital) → attracts money's energy form  
- **Tạo sản phẩm dịch vụ** (creating products/services) → enriches Vật chất (matter/assets) → attracts money's material form

Project setup is the upstream activity: a well-articulated project attracts the collaborators and resources needed for the other two forms of money attraction to follow.
