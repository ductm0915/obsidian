# Learning by Doing

**Learning by doing** is the pedagogical principle that real understanding comes from direct experience, not from observation or theoretical study alone.

## Vipassana Framework

This is precisely **Bhāvanā-maya-paññā** — the third and highest form of wisdom in Goenka's three-part framework:
1. Suta-maya-paññā — wisdom heard/read (from others)
2. Cintā-maya-paññā — wisdom through reasoning
3. **Bhāvanā-maya-paññā** — wisdom through direct personal experience

Only the third actually transforms behavior and habit patterns. See: [[bhavana-maya-panna]]
