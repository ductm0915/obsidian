# Lifecycle Ads

## What It Is

A system for generating marketing content — particularly testimonials and social proof — by mining recordings and touch points that *already exist* in a business's normal operations, without requiring founder-driven content creation.

## The Core Insight

Most businesses already record:
- Sales calls
- Onboarding calls
- Delivery checkpoints
- Client milestone moments

These recordings, stitched together, can be compressed into a **lifecycle timeline ad** — showing the prospect's journey from hesitation at the sales call through every touchpoint to transformation at the end. Instead of a talking-head testimonial, you show the actual journey.

*"Instead of saying 'I was here and now I'm here' — show them actually being scared, hesitating before signing up, and then getting this amazing outcome."*

## The 7 Systems (Complete Set)

Hormozi's full list of founder-free marketing systems, of which lifecycle ads is one:

1. Screenshot community testimonials weekly → run as ads
2. Incentivized testimonials via service tier upgrades (double whammy: ascension + marketing)
3. **Lifecycle ads** — CRM mining → sales call + onboarding + touchpoints → timeline ad
4. Document key client milestone moments (reveals, scale moments, revenue milestones)
5. Pay customer support $5 per chat-format mini-testimonial captured
6. Convert existing Google/Yelp/Amazon/TrustAdvisor reviews into marketing assets
7. One-star review inversion (Liquid Death model: cast out wrong buyers, attract right ones)

Extended options: affiliate programs, TikTok Shop UGC whitelisted as paid ads.

## Why It Matters

All seven systems remove **[[key-man-risk]] from marketing** — the business no longer needs the founder's face to generate acquisition. Marketing becomes a system, not a personality.
