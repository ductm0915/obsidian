# 15 Khái Niệm Nguồn (15 Source Concepts)

**Origin:** [[wit-organization]], taught by [[hong-trieu-bao]]
**Core function:** The master toolkit for achieving [[bay-su-giau-toan-dien]] across all four life domains

---

## Summary

The "15 Khái Niệm Nguồn" (15 Source Concepts) is [[wit-organization]]'s comprehensive framework for mastering life. Each concept represents a category of knowledge or practice that, when understood and applied correctly, enables mastery within any of the four life domains: nội tâm (inner self), sức khỏe (health), mối quan hệ (relationships), and tài chính (finance).

The full list appears verbatim in the lời biết ơn (gratitude preface) of every THNK K36 session PDF, indicating its status as a foundational declaration. The word "nguồn" (source/root) signals that these are not surface-level tactics but the root-level principles from which all effective practice flows.

## How the Framework is Structured

The 15 concepts are organized from most fundamental to most practical:

**Foundational level (1–2):** Quy luật and nguyên lý are the universal laws and principles that operate whether or not a person knows them. Everything else is downstream. If you align with these, all the other 13 naturally fall into place. See [[wit-k07-quy-luat]] for the specific laws taught.

**Cognitive/structural level (3–7):** Chìa khoá (keys), công thức (formulas), phương pháp (methods), nguyên tắc (rules), and quan niệm chuẩn (correct worldviews) give structure to how one approaches problems and makes decisions. These are the "mental models" layer.

**Internal state level (8–10):** Tâm thái đúng (correct mental states), năng lực phù hợp (appropriate capabilities), and hệ quy chiếu (frames of reference) are the inner conditions that determine whether knowledge can be effectively applied. These link directly to [[tam-thuc-tu-duy-hanh-vi]].

**Meta level (11):** Khái niệm nguồn as a concept within itself — the ability to identify and work with source concepts across any domain.

**Environmental/operational level (12–15):** Mật mã (codes — probably domain-specific shorthand or patterns), môi trường (environment — what surrounds and shapes you), công cụ - phương tiện (tools and means — the practical instruments), and nghi thức - nghi lễ (rituals and ceremonies — practices that anchor learning and build culture).

---

## Application Across 4 Domains

The same 15 concepts are applied within each of the four life domains:

- **Nội tâm:** e.g., nguyên lý ánh sáng (principle of light) as the key principle; tâm thái an vui as the correct mental state; ghi nhận biết ơn (grateful acknowledgment) as the daily ritual
- **Sức khỏe:** specific principles of body care; rituals like regular gratitude for one's physical body
- **Mối quan hệ:** quy luật chuyển hóa (law of transformation by love); tâm thái bao dung (tolerant mental state) as the key
- **Tài chính:** specific laws of how money flows; financial consciousness as the required tâm thức

## The Significance of "Nguồn" (Source)

The word "nguồn" (source/origin) is deliberately chosen. These are not tips or techniques but the generative roots from which all effective practices grow. A person who has "thấu suốt" (thoroughly understood) these 15 concepts within a domain can derive and invent appropriate practices for any situation, rather than depending on prescriptive instructions.

The program's goal is stated explicitly: *"giúp chúng em làm chủ cuộc đời thông qua giúp chúng em thấu suốt 15 khái niệm nguồn"* (help us take ownership of our lives by helping us thoroughly understand the 15 source concepts). "Làm chủ" (mastery, ownership) rather than "học thuộc" (memorization) is the distinction.

---

## Connection to [[nhan-thuc-du-day]]

Having [[nhan-thuc-du-day]] (sufficient awareness) about these 15 concepts, applied to each of the 4 domains, is the definition of complete personal mastery in WIT's framework. The three-level awareness model applies to each concept within each domain separately — a person may have complete awareness of quy luật in the domain of nội tâm but only partial awareness of quy luật in the domain of tài chính.
