# Hedonic Treadmill

The **hedonic treadmill** is the metaphor for hedonic adaptation: we run faster and faster (pursue more, achieve more) but our happiness level stays the same — as if on a treadmill.

See: [[hedonic-adaptation]] for full treatment.

## Vipassana Exit Strategy

Vipassana offers the only genuine exit: not acquiring better objects of desire, but **observing the craving itself** arise and pass. Once the meditator directly perceives that craving (tanha) is itself the suffering, not the absence of its object, the treadmill logic collapses.
