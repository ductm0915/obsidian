# Equanimity With Sensation (Upekkha)

**Equanimity (tâm xả, Pali: upekkha)** trong context Vipassana cụ thể có nghĩa: **không phản ứng với body sensations** — dù pleasant hay unpleasant. Đây là kỹ năng cốt lõi của [[body-sensation-junction]] và là trái tim của thực hành Vipassana theo [[sn-goenka]].

> *"Training your mind to remain equanimous — when everything is very pleasant, no pain, you say 'I'm very equanimous, look I'm not reacting.' When a real pain starts and you are equanimous — even for a few moments — those moments are so strong."*

---

## Equanimity Không Phải Là Suppression

- Không phải "cố không cảm thấy" hay "nén lại"
- Không phải tê liệt hay thờ ơ
- **Equanimous = observe fully + không generate craving hay aversion**
- "You observe it, don't react to it — just observe, just observe"

---

## Tại Sao Equanimity Quan Trọng?

### Vì Sensation Là Bất Biến (Anicca)

> *"How unpleasant a sensation may be — it is bound to pass away. How pleasant a sensation may be — it is bound to pass away."*

- Mọi sensation đều **arise and pass away** — đây là bản chất
- Nếu biết nó sẽ pass → reacting có ý nghĩa gì?
- "No purpose is served to react to something which is by nature passing away"

### Equanimity Breaks The Chain

Từ [[dependent-origination]]:
- Sensation without reaction → not tanha (craving) → not clinging → not becoming → not rebirth → **not misery**
- Ngược lại: reaction → multiply misery

---

## Levels Của Equanimity (Progression)

### Level 0: Full Reaction (Habitual Pattern)
- Mọi sensation → automatic reaction
- Old deepest habit pattern: pleasant → crave; unpleasant → avert
- "This has become a habit pattern of the deepest level of the mind — always a prisoner of it"

### Level 1: Vài Khoảnh Khắc Equanimous
*"Those few moments will turn into few seconds, they will turn into few minutes."*

- Trong 1 giờ ngồi: chỉ vài giây không react thực sự
- Những giây đó rất powerful — "starts cutting the root of your problem"
- Đây là realistic goal cho beginner

### Level 2: Start Enjoying Pain
*"One student comes and tells me: 'I now started enjoying my pain.' You start enjoying your pain because you stopped reacting to it."*

- Physical pain vẫn còn → nhưng không có mental multiplier
- Pain → quarter → less and less

### Level 3: Undercurrent of Vibration
- Khi dissect sensation sâu đủ: pressure, heat, pulsation
- Finally: **undercurrent of vibration throughout the body**
- "Once you reach that stage — pain is no pain for you, it cannot overpower you"

### Level 4: Full Equanimity (Liberation)
- Mọi sankara được eradicate
- Craving rooted out: không trace nào còn lại
- "Builder of the house" — ignorance/craving — destroyed

---

## Equanimity Vs. Intellectual Understanding

Goenka nhấn mạnh repeatedly:

> *"At the intellectual level one understands that I should not generate negativity — every negativity goes against my own peace, my own harmony, it is no good. But all that understanding of the intellect does not help this deepest level of the mind."*

- Intellectual understanding của equanimity ≠ equanimous at deepest level
- Chỉ **practice** mới thực sự rewire deepest level
- Barrier giữa surface mind và deepest level chỉ vỡ qua thực hành, không qua lý thuyết

---

## Practical Training: Ngồi 1 Tiếng

Goenka dùng ví dụ 1 giờ sitting:

```
0-30 min    : đa số manage được
30-45 min   : pain bắt đầu, "somehow manage"
45-60 min   : "real torture" — mỗi phút như 1 giờ
              → ĐÂY LÀ TRAINING GROUND
Khi chant   : hỷ lạc = "experience of liberation"
```

> *"You're learning now — not only that, the equanimity that you are training... if you are equanimous even for a few moments, rest of the time you're reacting. Those few moments of equanimity are so strong."*
