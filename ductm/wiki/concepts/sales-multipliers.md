# Sales Multipliers

## Definition
Sales multipliers are systemic optimizations and behaviors that exponentially increase the volume and conversion of a sales operation without necessarily increasing headcount. 

## Key Multipliers
According to [[alex-hormozi]]:
1. **Speed to Lead**: Responding in under 1 minute.
2. **7 Days a Week Availability**: Running operations through weekends capitalizes on high buyer activity and intent.
3. **15-Minute Scheduling Blocks**: Increasing appointment density over the traditional 30 or 60-minute blocks creates artificial urgency and fits more calls into a day.

## Application
These multipliers hinge on extreme operational efficiency. The premise is that small logistical edges compound massively over hundreds of leads.

## 13 Traits of Elite Salespeople (from [[hormozi-sell-better-99]])

Grouped into three buckets:

### Maximize Opportunities
1. **Maximum hours / days available** — Top performers work the most. Availability is the #1 predictor of total sales output.
2. **Pull up calls** — Move Thursday appointments to today; fill every open slot with the hottest available lead.
3. **On-call + off-call SOPs** — Two separate checklists: what you do *on* calls vs. what you do *between* calls. Most companies only have one.
4. **[[bamfam]]** — Never end a call without booking the next one. Zero lead dropoff.
5. **Don't take rejection personally** — Volume outreach: call leads more times than mediocre reps dare.
6. **Kill list** — A visible secondary list of whale prospects tracked outside the CRM. Always top of mind.
7. **Create own opportunities (referrals)** — "Who do you know who's as awesome as you?" Referral close rate is 80–90%; asking it adds ~50% to sales.

### Maximize Conversion Rate
8. **Prepare for every call** — 5–10% of call duration in research. "You win through prep when you don't have reputation yet."
9. **Take notes** — Build a profile across calls; restating their situation on the next call creates instant trust.
10. **Listen 2:1** — Elite reps listen twice as much as they talk. "The person answering questions is the one getting interrogated."
11. **Kill zombies up front** — Address obstacles before asking for money. It's always easier to defuse the bomb before the timer starts.
12. **Have key stories/metaphors** — One for each objection type. Stories break beliefs; features don't.
13. **Breathe the script** — Internalize it so completely you can be fully present. Can't listen while trying to remember what's next.

### Sustain Performance (Meta-Skills)
- **Kill for sport** — Tigers vs. horses vs. dogs. Best reps close every lead like it matters, even "garbage" leads (free practice with stakes).
- **Track metrics** — Close rate is a lagging indicator. Track: schedule rate, show rate, offer rate, close rate, average call length, cash collected. Skill level correlates to quality of metrics tracked.
- **Never blame circumstances** — Dogs blame leads; tigers are grateful for the practice.

## Sales Team Optimization (Case Study)
Real results from fixing a broken team in 60 days:
- Show rate: 49% → 84% (70% improvement)
- Close rate: 27% → 41% (50% improvement)
- Cash collected upfront: 47% → 82%
- Overall sales: 56 → 93/month

Fixes: better ad targeting, fewer/better reps, dedicated lead nurture specialist, deeper discovery script, looping training.
