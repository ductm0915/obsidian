# Hormozi — 6 Levels of Making Money

See source: [[hormozi-6-levels-making-money]]

The six compensation structures, ranked by risk borne and corresponding upside:

| Level | Structure | Risk | Upside |
|-------|-----------|------|--------|
| 1 | W2 Employment | Lowest | Capped |
| 2 | Self-employed / Freelancer | Low-Medium | Semi-capped |
| 3 | Business Owner (team) | Medium | Uncapped |
| 4 | Equity / Ownership stake | High | Asymmetric |
| 5 | Investor (capital deployment) | Variable | Scalable |
| 6 | Tax collector / Risk seller | Structural | Institutional |

## Core Thesis

> "The higher the level, the more of the downside risk you bear. But the upside becomes non-linear." — Hormozi

Mispriced bets (opportunities where the market undervalues the risk/reward) are the path to outsized returns at each level.
