# State Management

**State management** is the practice of deliberately choosing and maintaining one's internal emotional/mental state — rather than being driven by external circumstances.

## WIT Framework (Inner State)

WIT defines state management as the central leadership competency: a leader who cannot manage their own state cannot effectively lead others. See: [[inner-state]]

## Vipassana Framework

Vipassana offers a precise mechanism for state management:
1. All states manifest as body sensations (vedanā)
2. Observe sensations without reacting
3. Equanimity = the "master state" — not a specific emotion but the capacity to remain stable through any

## Key Principle

State management ≠ emotional suppression. It is:
- Awareness that a state has arisen (sampajañña)
- Choice not to amplify or suppress it (upekkhā)
- Natural return to baseline
