# Āsava — Mental Fermentations/Outflows

Āsava (Pali: āsava = outflows, fermentations, or biochemical secretions of the mind) are the mental-physical flows generated by defilements. Goenka explains these as a biochemical vicious circle: a defilement arises → generates a body sensation → the sensation stimulates more of the same defilement → more sensation → etc. This is the mechanism of addiction (craving feeds on itself). Vipassana breaks this cycle by observing the sensation with equanimity, starving the feedback loop. Three types: kāmāsava (sensual craving), bhavāsava (craving for existence), avijjāsava (ignorance).
