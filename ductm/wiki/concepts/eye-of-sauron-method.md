# Eye of Sauron Method

The **Eye of Sauron Method** is [[caleb-ralston]]'s approach to managing multiple content platforms by focusing innovation energy on one platform at a time while keeping others on maintenance mode.

## How It Works

1. **Choose 3 primary platforms** (e.g., YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn).
2. **Focus the "eye"** on one platform — all time, attention, money, and innovation goes here.
3. **Maintenance mode** for the other platforms — still posting, using known formats that work, but no active innovation or experimentation.
4. **Rotate** once the focused platform is "dialed in." Move the eye to the next platform.
5. **Loop back** — by the time you return to the first platform, your audience has evolved and you'll need to innovate again.

## Caleb's Application
- Year 1: Eye on YouTube. Instagram and LinkedIn on maintenance mode.
- YouTube got all innovation budget and experimentation.
- Instagram/LinkedIn posted repurposed content in proven formats.

## Why It Works
- Prevents the "omnipresence trap" — trying to innovate on all platforms simultaneously dilutes quality everywhere.
- Audience preferences constantly evolve; the rotating eye ensures continuous adaptation.
- Named after the Eye of Sauron from Lord of the Rings: it focuses intensely on one area at a time.
