# Volume Negates Luck

## Definition
The principle that engaging in an extreme, overwhelming amount of repetition eliminates the variability of "luck" or initial "talent" in achieving an outcome. 

## Application
Coined frequently in business and famously emphasized by [[alex-hormozi]] in go-to-market operations. 
In sales, it implies that forcing yourself or your team into a massive funnel of leads and reps guarantees an eventual statistical success. The more you do, the better you get (skill acceleration); the better you get, the more efficiently you close.

Rather than looking to score the perfect lead, an practitioner of this concept welcomes all leads ("be a trashman") simply to acquire the repetitions necessary for mastery.
