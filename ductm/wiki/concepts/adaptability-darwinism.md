# Adaptability Darwinism

**Adaptability Darwinism** is the business strategy principle — drawing on evolutionary biology — that in changing environments, **the most flexible system survives**, not the strongest or largest.

Darwin's insight: survival advantage goes to organisms that can adapt quickly to environmental shifts. Applied to business: companies/people who can shift strategy without losing core identity outlast those rigidly committed to a single model.

## Hormozi Application

Hormozi uses this frame in discussing game theory and competitive strategy: the player who can rapidly iterate their offering, message, or channel based on feedback will outcompete one who "stays the course" regardless of results.
