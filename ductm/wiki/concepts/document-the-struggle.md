# Document The Struggle

The practice of purposefully recording the early, often embarrassing, stages of building a business or personal brand, rather than waiting until success has been achieved.

Many individuals intentionally avoid documenting their beginnings due to shame or a desire to only present themselves as polished experts. However, documenting the struggle serves as an authentic artifact that creates a more compelling narrative later (the "comeback" or "origin" story).

It also acts as an active demonstration of conviction: documenting early signals an unwavering belief that the individual will eventually win.
