# Công Thức Tạo Cộng Đồng Chuyên Gia

**Translation:** Formula for Creating an Expert Community
**Origin:** [[wit-organization]], taught by [[vu-phuong]] in Mentor K07
**Source:** [[wit-k07-cong-thuc]]

---

## Core Idea

WIT's expert community formula is a systematic blueprint for building communities of genuine transformation — not audiences, subscribers, or customer databases. The "chuyên gia" (expert) qualifier signals a specific ambition: communities where participants are developing into experts themselves, not merely consuming content.

The formula is built on the insight that communities form around shared reality, not shared information. The leader's own cảnh giới cuộc sống (inner state / life quality) is the primary attractor. Without this, no formula works.

Compare: [[community-vs-audience]] — this is the WIT-native articulation of the same distinction.

---

## The Three Levels of Community Alignment

The formula targets three progressive depths of connection, from easiest to hardest:

| Level | Vietnamese | Meaning | Scale Target |
|-------|-----------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Đồng ngôn | Same language / same concepts | ~20 people |
| 2 | Đồng thuận | Same conviction / same agreement | ~500 people |
| 3 | Đồng hành | Same path / walking together | ~10,000 people |

"Đồng ngôn" means people share the same vocabulary and conceptual framework. "Đồng thuận" means they have personally verified and agreed to the underlying values. "Đồng hành" means they are actively co-creating together.

Most communities achieve only đồng ngôn (shared language). The Công Thức aims for all three. Without the deeper levels, a community dissolves when the leader is absent or the novelty fades.

## The Formula Structure

### Prerequisite: Cảnh Giới Cuộc Sống (Inner State)

Before any formula step, the leader must be a living example. The statement: "Nói không bao giờ trở thành được hết" (Words alone never transform anyone). The three dimensions of inner state that must be developed:

1. **Giàu trí tuệ** (wisdom-rich) — knowing which level of knowledge applies when; able to navigate between levels 1–4 of WIT's wisdom taxonomy
2. **Giàu tâm thái** (inner disposition-rich) — genuine peace, gratitude, and joy as default states rather than performances
3. **Giàu nhân cách** (character-rich) — the nine traits of complete character: vui vẻ, hy vọng, niềm tin, trí tuệ, trân trọng biết ơn, yêu thương, bao dung, khiêm tốn, chân thật

This prerequisite explains why WIT's community-building curriculum begins with inner-life development, not marketing tactics.

### Step 1: Start From Yourself (Từ Chính Mình)

The community starts as a single point: you. The leader must:
- Define their cố định quả (fixed outcome) — the specific vision of what they and their community are becoming
- Operate from a state of genuine trân trọng biết ơn (appreciation and gratitude) as the baseline energy
- Be willing to be seen as a work-in-progress rather than projecting false completion

Cô Vũ Phương: "Ai cũng muốn thu hút con người ở gần mình nhưng mà làm thế nào để thu hút và giữ chân là phát triển được họ đó" (Everyone wants to attract people, but the art is attracting, retaining, and developing them).

### Step 2: The Triad — Lãnh Đạo + Tả Phù + Hữu Bật

Three people form the minimum viable community structure:

**Lãnh đạo (Leader)**
- Holds the vision and direction
- Operates from kiện toàn nhân cách (complete character)
- Dẫn dắt (leads with wisdom) both Tả Phù and Hữu Bật
- Is not the sole point of energy — their role is to create conditions, not to do everything

**Tả Phù (Left Supporter — Internal)**
- Handles operations, resources, execution
- The "đôi bàn tay và đôi chân" (hands and feet) of the leader
- Translates the leader's vision into concrete plans and execution
- Manages internal coordination

**Hữu Bật (Right Supporter — External)**
- Handles quảng bá (broadcasting), outward communication
- The "đôi mắt và miệng" (eyes and voice) of the leader
- Represents the community to the outside world
- Attracts new members and maintains external relationships

Geometric principle: One point = a dot. Two points = a line (no bounded space). Three points = a plane — minimum to form an enclosed, self-sustaining environment. This is why three people are the structural minimum, not two.

### Step 3: Quảng Bá, Phối Hợp, Dẫn Dắt (The Three Operational Pillars)

Once the triad is established, the community grows through:

**Quảng bá** (Broadcasting/Promotion)
Not marketing — sharing transformation stories. Quảng bá activates nhu cầu ẩn (latent needs) in others by showing them what is possible. The key question: what does complete success look like? Broadcast that picture.

**Phối hợp** (Coordination)
The triad works in synchronized alignment. The leader doesn't micromanage but creates conditions where Tả Phù and Hữu Bật can act with full authority in their domains.

**Dẫn dắt** (Guidance/Leadership)
Consistent wisdom-based direction that maintains the community's values and direction over time. This is ongoing — not a one-time founding act.

### Step 4: Expansion — The Fractal Model

Each member who fully develops through the community can become a leader with their own triad. The formula is fractal:

1 leader → 2 supporters (triad)
Each supporter → can grow their own triad
Pattern: 1 → 3 → 9 → 27 → ...

WIT's institutional targets: 500 Masters → 10,000 Mentors → 100,000 Doanh Chủ → 1 million Nhà Giáo Dục Gia Đình

## The 6 Đồng (Lục Đồng) Extension

Referenced as "Lục đồng làm nên sự nghiệp" (Six alignments build the enterprise), this extends the concept of đồng ngôn/thuận/hành with three additional dimensions of alignment. Full details in WIT's deeper organizational teachings.

## Relationship to Other Concepts

The Công Thức operates as the outward expression of the individual leader's [[tong-nghiep-nhu-y]] (karma alignment). A person who has activated their tổng nghiệp như ý naturally attracts the right triad, creates the right environment, and generates genuine community.

The "quả của duyên" (relationship fruit) in the karma formula maps directly onto đồng ngôn, đồng thuận, đồng hành.
