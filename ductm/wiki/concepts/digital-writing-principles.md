# Digital Writing Principles

**Digital writing principles** govern how to write effectively for online audiences — where attention is scarce, skimming is the norm, and the first line determines whether anyone reads further.

## Core Principles (Ship 30 / Nicholas Cole Framework)

1. **1-1-1 Rule**: One idea, one reader, one transformation per piece
2. **The First Line Rule**: Your first line competes with everything else on the internet — it must stop the scroll
3. **Formatting for skimmers**: Short paragraphs, white space, one idea per paragraph
4. **Specificity over generality**: Specific claims build credibility; vague claims lose it
5. **Prep the Page** (Cole): Setup the reader's expectations before delivering substance
