# Quy Trình Thành Công Của Nhà Lãnh Đạo Siêu Phàm

**Translation:** Success Process of Extraordinary Leaders
**Origin:** [[wit-organization]], taught by Thầy Phạm Ngọc Tuấn Anh in Mentor K07
**Source:** [[wit-k07-qui-trinh]]

---

## Core Idea

This is WIT's master success blueprint — a repeatable, systematic process applicable to individuals, families, organizations, and businesses. It is framed as "extraordinary" (siêu phàm) because it requires thinking, acting, and living in ways most people will not:

> "Dám nghĩ điều mà người khác không dám nghĩ. Dám làm điều mà người khác không dám làm. Và kết quả đó là mình sẽ hưởng những điều mà người khác không hưởng được."
> (Dare to think what others won't. Dare to do what others won't. The result: enjoy what others cannot.)

The meta-premise: "Mọi thành công viên mãn bắt nguồn từ môi trường tốt" — All complete success originates from a good environment.

---

## Why "Success Process" Not "Success Formula"

A process implies a sequence that can be learned, practiced, and improved — not a one-time insight. WIT uses quy trình deliberately: the same process applies whether building a personal brand, a business, a family culture, or a 10,000-person community. The steps are the same; only the context changes.

## The Process: Four Layers

### Layer 1 — Build the Inner Foundation (Cá Nhân)

Before any outward action, three internal capacities must be developed:

**Quan Niệm Chuẩn (Correct Worldview/Concepts)**
The most foundational layer. Everything downstream depends on whether your mental models of reality are accurate. WIT teaches a set of 9 core worldview concepts (chín quan niệm chuẩn) for transformational consultants. Critical examples:
- "Vốn của con người là con người" — Your primary asset is people, not money or tools
- "Tiền mình có được tỷ lệ thuận với số lượng và chất lượng người mình giúp đỡ" — Income is proportional to the quantity and quality of people you help
- "Không là ai thì có thể trở thành bất kỳ ai" — Not being fixed in an identity enables becoming anything

**Tâm Thái Đúng (Correct Inner Disposition)**
Right worldview naturally produces right inner states. When you genuinely believe your capital is people, every person who approaches becomes an opportunity to serve — not an interruption or a sale to be made. Tâm thái (inner disposition) is not a mood; it's the persistent orientation from which you operate.

**Năng Lực Phù Hợp (Appropriate Capability)**
The skills to deliver value. WIT distinguishes:
- Năng lực nền tảng (foundational capacity): quảng bá (broadcasting), phối hợp (coordination), dẫn dắt (leadership guidance) — taught within WIT
- Năng lực chuyên môn (professional expertise): each person's domain-specific skills (education, healthcare, business, etc.)

Note: AI tools are explicitly encouraged as a way to expand capability rapidly — "Thông qua AI thì gần như là toàn bộ tri thức liên quan đến thế giới quan nó nằm ở trong đó" (Via AI, virtually all world-knowledge is accessible).

### Layer 2 — Create the Good Environment (Môi Trường Tốt)

A good environment (môi trường tốt) is defined as:

> "Môi trường mà con người cảm thấy thoải mái và được khuyến khích phát triển toàn diện"
> (An environment where people feel comfortable and are encouraged to develop holistically)

Nine characteristics of a good environment (mirroring the nine traits of complete character):
**Vui vẻ** (joyful), **hy vọng** (hopeful), **niềm tin** (trusting), **trí tuệ** (wise), **trân trọng biết ơn** (grateful), **yêu thương** (loving), **bao dung** (tolerant), **khiêm tốn** (humble), **chân thật** (authentic)

**Minimum structure:** Three people with aligned intent (the triad from [[cong-thuc-cong-dong-chuyen-gia]]):
- One leader with kiện toàn nhân cách (complete character)
- One Tả Phù (internal operations)
- One Hữu Bật (external relations/broadcasting)

Three create a "diện" (plane/enclosed space) — the minimum for a sustainable environment. Two only create a "tuyến" (line) — insufficient for self-sustaining community.

### Layer 3 — The Attraction → Integration Cycle

Once the environment exists, the process cycles:

**1. Quảng bá thành công viên mãn (Broadcast the success vision)**
Share what complete success looks like — the 16 Chân Dung (16 character portraits), the dream life, the transformation outcomes. This activates nhu cầu ẩn (latent needs) in those who hear it.

The demand curve: latent need → active need → accepting a solution → becoming a customer → becoming an advocate. The leader's job is to activate latent needs; the environment does the conversion.

**2. Con người đến (People arrive)**
Those with activated needs approach. They are at various stages of readiness — some just curious, some ready to commit.

**3. Đưa vào môi trường (Bring into the environment)**
The environment does the work: people absorb correct worldview, develop right inner disposition, and build appropriate capability simply by being in the environment and practicing.

**4. Dân số tăng (Population grows)**
Members who develop fully become the next generation of leaders — each capable of forming their own triad, extending the process fractally.

### Layer 4 — Scale and Organizational Longevity (Tổ Chức Trường Tồn)

The fully realized outcome of the process is a tổ chức trường tồn (enduring organization) or doanh nghiệp trường tồn (enduring enterprise):

> "Tổ chức tồn tại và phát triển qua nhiều thế hệ, có đặc tính của gia đình, tôn giáo, tín ngưỡng và đạo lý."
> (An organization that exists and develops across many generations, carrying the characteristics of family, religion, conviction, and ethics.)

Three foundational characteristics: **gia đình** (family), **đạo lý** (ethics/principles), **tôn giáo/tín ngưỡng** (spiritual conviction).

WIT's organizational math: 10,000 Mentors × capability to each reach their own network → 100,000 business leaders → 1 million family educators → systemic societal transformation.

## The 16 Chân Dung (Success Target)

The concrete personal achievement target for every WIT Mentor — read as daily affirmation:

1. Trí tuệ học giả (scholar's wisdom)
2. Tâm thái an vui (peaceful inner disposition)
3. Nhân cách kiện toàn (complete character)
4. Phẩm chất ưu tú (outstanding quality)
5. Năng lực xuất chúng (exceptional capability)
6. Thân hình người mẫu (model physique)
7. Quảng bá siêu phàm (extraordinary broadcasting)
8. Giao tiếp thông thái (wise communication)
9. Luật sắc bản thân (mastery of self-refinement)
10. Tầm nhìn thấu suốt (penetrating vision)
11. Thấu hiểu nhân sinh (deep understanding of the human condition)
12. Bác ái lĩnh chúng (benevolent leadership of people)
13. Đức hàng thiên hạ (virtue that transcends ordinary limits)
14. Lục lộc đại thuận (great abundance in all six domains)
15. Lãnh đạo toàn năng (omnipotent leadership / becoming a cultural entrepreneur)

## Key Principles

**Doanh chủ xây dựng con người, con người xây dựng kinh doanh**
Business leaders build people; people build the business. Counterintuitive for most entrepreneurs: focus on people development, not business KPIs. The business follows.

**Phần chìm của tảng băng**
The "submerged part of the iceberg" — trí tuệ, tâm thái, nhân cách, phẩm chất, năng lực, thể chất are the invisible foundation. Only doanh số (sales volume), thành tích (achievements), and thành công (success) are visible. WIT teaches the invisible foundation.

**Kích hoạt nhu cầu ẩn**
Never push; activate. The leader's role is to reveal to people what they already want but haven't articulated. Quảng bá (broadcasting transformation stories) is the primary activation mechanism.
