# Tài Chính — Kinh Doanh — Đầu Tư Framework (Finance-Business-Investment Framework)

WIT's integrated view of finance (tài chính), business (kinh doanh), and investment (đầu tư) as three interconnected systems governed by universal laws and principles — not separate domains requiring separate tactics. Developed primarily by [[hong-trieu-bao]] in the [[wit-k07-tc-kd-dt]] module (May 2024) and applied practically in [[wit-k07-ung-dung-amway]]. The consciousness foundation is in [[nhan-thuc-ve-tien]].

Core claim: finance, business, and investment all operate according to the same underlying laws. Mastering the laws produces results across all three domains. Tactics without law-alignment produce short-term gains at the cost of long-term depletion.

## The Three Laws

### 1. Tam Giác Giới (Three-World Triangle Law)

The structural law governing sustainable business and wealth creation. Any enterprise must harmonize three stakeholder groups:

```
        Chính phủ (Government)
            Information / Legitimacy
                    △
                   /|\
                  / | \
                 /  |  \
    Người giàu ----+---- Người dân
    (Wealthy)    DOANH  (People)
   Energy/Capital NHÂN  Physical/Market
```

**Operating rule**: Position yourself at the center, serving all three without extracting from any.

| Stakeholder | What They Represent | How to Honor |
|---|---|---|
| Chính phủ | Information, policy, institutional legitimacy | Do not circumvent regulations; increase trust between government and people |
| Người giàu | Monetary energy, investment capital | Do not directly seek their money; let your track record attract their support |
| Người dân | Physical market, actual human needs | Create genuine value; this is the primary mandate |

"Người làm tăng niềm tin của dân chúng đối với chính phủ thì chính là người đang sống thuận Tam Giác Giới." (Whoever increases the people's trust in government is living in harmony with the three-world triangle.)

The three releases required to operate in harmony:
- **Buông danh** — release reputation-seeking
- **Buông tài** — release personal financial extraction
- **Buông tham tưởng cá nhân** — release personal ego/agenda

Paradox: releasing these three things is what attracts all three stakeholders to support you. This is the practical expression of ý niệm dương (yang/giving intention) at the business level.

**Practical test for any project**: Does it align with the interests of government, wealthy people (as a class, not specific individuals), and the general population simultaneously? If yes → thuận tam giác giới → proceed.

### 2. Phước Báu Law (Merit/Karma Law)

Applied to business and investment:

- Value created > money received → merit (phước) accumulates → capacity for larger wealth grows
- Money received > value created → merit depletes → wealth becomes unstable

This is not merely ethical guidance but causal mechanism. WIT's framing: phước is the energy substrate from which financial results emerge. Building phước by consistently under-receiving relative to value created creates the energetic conditions for exponential financial growth later.

In investment terms: choose investments that create genuine social value, not just financial extraction. Projects that are lâu — lớn — lành (durable, large, wholesome) generate phước while generating returns.

### 3. Ý Niệm Dương (Yang Intention Law)

From Thầy Bảo: "Tiền là âm." Money has yin (receptive, material) energy. To attract yin energy, one must embody yang (active, giving, outward) energy.

"Ai làm điều gì vì lợi ích của người khác thì thường sẽ đơn giản để thu hút tiền hơn là người làm vì chính mình."

At the business level: enterprises motivated by genuine service to others attract money more efficiently than enterprises motivated by extraction. This is not about appearing altruistic — the intention must be genuine and verifiable through internal liêm chính (integrity).

Formula: **Tâm giúp người + Tâm thấu suốt = Thu hút tài chính bền vững**
(Heart of helping + Penetrating understanding = Sustainable financial attraction)

## Project Framework: Lâu — Lớn — Lành

Every business project should satisfy three quality criteria:

| Criterion | Meaning | Business Application |
|---|---|---|
| **Lâu** (Durable) | Designed to last generations | 49-year horizon (WIT's model); not a quick-turn project |
| **Lớn** (Large) | Scalable impact beyond one person | Network effects, community, systems that grow |
| **Lành** (Wholesome) | Ethically clean, no hidden costs | Transparent, thuận tam giác giới, no karmic debt |

## Resource Framework: Tâm — Tài — Lực

Before starting any project, assess three resource categories:

- **Tâm** (Heart/Intention) — Is the motivation correct? Is it vì người (for others), not vì tài danh (for money/fame)?
- **Tài** (Financial Resources) — Sufficient to begin and sustain; does not require extracting from others
- **Lực** (Capacity) — Skills, relationships, operational capacity to execute

Projects that lack any one of the three should be developed before launching. A project can typically begin within 6 months of clarifying all three.

## Project Lifecycle

**Start**: When tâm — tài — lực are in place and project is thuận tam giác giới.

**Completion**: When approximately 200 people are:
- **Đồng ngôn** (same language/vocabulary — shared conceptual framework)
- **Đồng thuận** (aligned — shared goals and values)
- **Đồng hành** (actively accompanying — committed participants)

This milestone (200 true co-travelers) indicates the project has achieved critical mass and self-sustaining momentum. "Thành công viên mãn" (complete/fulfilled success) at this point.

## The Six Financial Accounts (Sáu Tài Khoản Tài Chính)

Referenced in sessions (taught by Cô Hoàng Anh; full content not in available transcripts). The structural framework for allocating income across:

1. Necessary expenses (chi tiêu thiết yếu)
2. Investment/financial freedom account
3. Education/self-development
4. Long-term savings
5. Play/enjoyment
6. Giving/contribution

These mirror T. Harv Eker's "Jars" allocation system but WIT adds a seventh "account": the **tài khoản trống hồ tâm** (empty-mind/clear-consciousness account) — an inner resource of mental clarity and open awareness that determines whether the outer six accounts function properly.

The inner account is the prerequisite; without it, the six financial accounts become mechanical rules rather than living practices.

## The "Hút Tiền" vs "Kiếm Tiền" Distinction

Central to the module's practical framing:

- **Kiếm tiền** (earn/hunt money) — exchanging labor for wages; transactional; limited by hours and effort; no compounding
- **Hút tiền** (attract money) — having a project and vision that money flows toward; creates pull; compounds through networks and reputation

The shift from kiếm to hút requires:
1. A meaningful project (not just a job)
2. A clear spending plan with purpose (kế hoạch xài tiền có ý nghĩa)
3. Building money-as-information and money-as-energy before physical accumulation
4. Alignment with tam giác giới

"Người đi kiếm tiền là người chỉ chăm chăm kiếm việc để làm, đổ mồ hôi nhưng tiền lại chẳng ở lâu. Còn người hút tiền thì họ có dự án, có tầm nhìn."

## The Value Exchange Principle (Trả Giá)

What can legitimately be exchanged in the marketplace?

Thầy Bảo: Only what truly belongs to you — what you would carry beyond this lifetime. In practical terms: your accumulated wisdom, unique frameworks, and the distinctive value created through your specific life experiences (nghiệp thức — karmic consciousness).

Exchange formula: Identify your unique capital → find the corresponding social need → offer it authentically → receive money as the feedback signal of value created.

"Chỉ có cái thật sự thuộc về mình thì mình mới có thể dùng để trao đổi cho điều mình mong muốn."

This applies at multiple scales:
- Individual: exchange your unique skills and knowledge, not borrowed formulas
- WIT as an organization: exchange "triết lý giáo dục tận gốc" (root educational philosophy) for financial support
- Student businesses: identify their specific accumulated knowledge/experience as tradeable asset

## Integrated Model: Finance — Business — Investment

WIT treats these three as one system with three temporal phases:

| Phase | Domain | Primary Activity |
|---|---|---|
| Foundation | **Tài chính** (Finance) | Build consciousness about money; establish correct intention; create spending plan with meaning |
| Creation | **Kinh doanh** (Business) | Build projects thuận tam giác giới; create value for dân; build lâu lớn lành enterprises |
| Multiplication | **Đầu tư** (Investment) | Deploy accumulated phước and capital into expanding projects; let money-as-information compound |

Each stage depends on the previous. Investment without strong business consciousness produces unstable returns. Business without correct financial consciousness produces burnout and extraction. Financial consciousness without a project remains theoretical.
