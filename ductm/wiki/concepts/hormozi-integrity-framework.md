# Hormozi Integrity Framework

Hormozi's framework for integrity centers on the gap between **stated values** and **demonstrated behavior** — integrity is defined as the size of this gap.

## Core Principle

> "Integrity is doing what you said you would do, when you said you would do it, at the quality you said you would do it." — Hormozi

This creates an operational definition divorced from morality — integrity is a reliability metric.

## Why Integrity Compounds

1. **Trust** = the most valuable business asset (hardest to build, easiest to destroy)
2. Each integrity violation creates a small credibility debt
3. Each integrity fulfillment creates a compound credibility credit
4. At scale: your reputation is your balance sheet

## Application to Personal Brand

Hormozi treats personal integrity as the foundation of personal brand:
- What you say you'll do = brand promise
- What you actually do = brand reality
- Gap = brand liability

The "No BS" ethos across Hormozi's work is an integrity signal: say less, deliver more.
