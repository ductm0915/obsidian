# Application Router

A smart routing mechanism inside an apply funnel that automatically directs prospects to the **correct offer tier** based on their stage, goals, and background — rather than presenting a single offer to everyone.

From [[matt-gray]]'s Beautiful Business model, Phase 4.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Without a router, two failure modes occur:
1. **Overselling**: Beginners routed to high-ticket offers they're not ready for → bad fit, bad outcomes, refunds
2. **Underselling**: Advanced founders routed to entry-level products → wasted opportunity, low satisfaction

Both waste sales team time "meandering with people that are not right" for a given offer.

---

## How It Works

After a prospect arrives at the apply page (via content or workshop CTA), they fill out an application form. The logic engine reads their responses and routes them:

| Stage | Routed To |
|---|---|
| Just getting started | Entry-level offers ($29–$300) |
| 6-figure → 7-figure scaling | Core offers (Program, Velocity) |
| 7-figure → 8-figure | High-ticket (Edge: $100K+) |

The router uses "smart tech" — typically a quiz or application form with conditional logic (e.g., Typeform, Survey Funnel, or custom form).

---

## Benefits

- **For customers**: Efficiently gets them to the best product for their current situation
- **For the founder**: Eliminates wasted sales calls; protects high-ticket containers from mismatched buyers
- **For the business**: Maximizes close rate at each tier; improves downstream retention and outcomes

---

## Position in the Funnel

```
Content (social) → Newsletter / Workshop → Apply Page
                                                ↓
                                         Application Router
                                          ↙       ↓       ↘
                                       Entry    Core   High-Ticket
                                          ↓       ↓       ↓
                                         Sales Call → Onboarding
```

## Connection to Offer Stack Model
