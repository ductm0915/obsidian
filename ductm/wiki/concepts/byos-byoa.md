# BYOS / BYOA — Bring Your Own Software / Agent

## Definition

**BYOS** (Bring Your Own Software) and **BYOA** (Bring Your Own Agent/Agents) describes the emerging model where individuals embed their own trained AI infrastructure into businesses — functioning as entire departments rather than individual contributors.

Coined by [[alex-hormozi]] as the "medium-term future of business."

---

## The Core Proposition

> "I am your entire marketing department." — said by one person, backed by agents.

Instead of selling your time or expertise, you sell an *output* — the same output a full department would produce — but generated by agents you've trained and maintain. The employer gets the department; you collect the department budget.

**Example from the video:** Anthropic reportedly operated with 1 person in their marketing department — possible because that person deployed agents doing the bulk of the work.

---

## Value Capture Models

The BYOA operator has multiple extraction paths:

| Model | Description |
|---|---|
| Agency/contractor | Sell department-level output as a service |
| Embedded + equity | Embed in the company and take a slice of equity |
| Employed + premium cash | Join as a traditional employee but command outsized salary |

All three were previously impossible at this leverage ratio. AI makes all three viable for a single person.

---

## Why This Window Exists

Price sensitivity is slow to adapt. Businesses are used to paying $5K–$50K/month for departments. The *cost basis* of producing that output is collapsing toward zero. The pricing hasn't caught up yet. First movers capture the spread.

---

## Relationship to Revenue Per Employee

Hormozi's AI-native companies already operate at **millions per employee per year**. BYOA is the individual-level expression of the same dynamic — one person with the right agents can now generate value previously requiring 10–20 people.
