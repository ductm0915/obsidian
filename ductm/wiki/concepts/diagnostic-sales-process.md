# Diagnostic Sales Process

## Definition
A sales methodology by [[alex-hormozi]] that replaces transactional membership/package selling with a **diagnosis** of the prospect's specific gap — then prices to close that exact gap. Instead of "here's our $99/month plan," you say "here's what it takes to get you from X to Y, and here's the price."

## The Three-Step Framework

```
Current State  →  [Obstacle]  →  Desired State
     200 lbs                        140 lbs
```

1. **Current** — Where are they now? (Have them self-identify via a scale, chart, or questionnaire)
2. **Desired** — Where do they want to go? (Let them pick — they own the goal)
3. **Obstacle** — What's in between? (What they *think* is the obstacle is often not the real one)

## How It Converts to Price

1. Quantify the gap (e.g., 60 lbs to lose)
2. Calculate the timeline using your method (e.g., 1.5 lbs/week = 40 weeks)
3. Price tied to goal: "$99/week × 40 weeks = $3,960 to get you to 140 lbs guaranteed"
4. Add a guarantee: "If you follow the steps and don't reach your goal, I keep working with you for free"
5. Offer a prepay discount: "Save 10% if you prepay today = save $396"

Result: You're no longer selling a *membership* — you're selling **exactly what they said they want**, with a guaranteed outcome and a price tied to the result.

## Why This Works

> "Instead of selling a membership, I sell exactly what they want and put a price tag on it."

- Average revenue per customer jumped from $200 → $800 across a 32-location chain using this process alone.
- Yogurt Land principle: when *they* fill the cup, *they* own the decision. Same pricing — different psychology.
- Decision maker objection disappears: "You don't want filler; you want to look a certain way. You picked the goal, we priced the path to it."

## Pre-Sale Questionnaire
Collect pain before the conversation:
- What brought you in today?
- Current situation?
- How long dealing with this?
- If unresolved in 5 years, how much worse?
- Get the credit card *before* the pitch — "just completing your profile" (standard procedure framing)
