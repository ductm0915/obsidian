# Complete Content Strategy to Get Customers In 2026

[[caleb-ralston]] lays out a full content system that generated 2.8 billion views, 11M+ followers, and 650,000 leads in 2.5 years. The core thesis: for high-ticket offers, optimizing for trust beats optimizing for virality.

## Key Claims and Arguments

- **Viral Dilutes the Message**: Going viral requires wider content that attracts followers who never buy. Meanwhile, existing customers tune out because the content no longer serves them. Views go up, sales go down.
- **Trust = Belief in Future Delivery**: Trust is defined as how much you believe someone will meet expectations in the future based on past behavior. Build trust by repeatedly solving painful problems.
- **4-Step Trust Content Formula**: (1) Identify painful problems, (2) Define your unique solution, (3) Set expectations per video, (4) Make it easy to take action. Repeat → trust compounds.
- **Customer over Follower**: If you have a high-ticket offer, optimize content around customer feedback (DMs, emails, texts) not comment sections. Whoever you optimize for, you get more of.
- **Sustainable System First**: 73% of content creators quit within their first year. Build a system that fits your actual constraints (time, energy, experience). 20-minute daily walks > 6 months of Ronnie Coleman.
- **Choose Medium Naturally**: Four mediums — Video, Audio, Written, Graphic. Pick the one most natural to you, not the trending one. Video has highest leverage but only if you'll stick with it.
- **Platform Discipline**: Solo creator = 1 primary + 1 secondary platform. With team = 3 primary platforms. Never try to be omnipresent.
- **Eye of Sauron Method**: Focus innovation on one platform at a time; others go on maintenance mode. Rotate the eye continuously as audience preferences evolve.
- **Cadence = Marathon**: Start with realistic frequency and increase over time. Creators struggle to step back volume once set. 15 minutes of fame is worthless without long-term consistency.
- **Educational vs. Entertainment**: A client's views/subscribers grew but leads flatlined because they were making entertainment, not educational content. Switching to educational principles yielded 4x leads per video.

## Notable Quotes

> "When you optimize for virality, inherently you have to make content that is wider. And typically what this ends up doing is diluting the message."

> "Trust is how much you believe that someone or something will meet your expectations in the future based on how they have behaved in the past."

> "You'll get way greater returns doing 20-minute walks every day for the rest of your life than six months of Ronnie Coleman working out."
