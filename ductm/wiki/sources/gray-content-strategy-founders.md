## Unique Angles in This Version

While the framework is the same, this version has a few slightly different emphases worth noting:

### Organic Pre-Suasion

The most clearly articulated statement of why organic content outperforms paid ads for sales:

> "Organic leads arrive after months of following content — sometimes even a few years. When they come to a sales call, they're not skeptical. Their core emotion is actually a level of curiosity. They already trust us. There's already a level of pre-suasion that has convinced them via the content, so they don't need to be persuaded on a call. They just need to know which product is right for them."

This connects to [[signal-vs-noise-content]], [[optimize-for-trust]], and [[rented-vs-owned-audience]].

### Why Founders Quit (Longevity)

Direct diagnosis of the failure mode:

> "Most founders quit not because of lack of ambition — they quit because they build wrong: scattered audience, too many offers, disparate value props. They spin their tires, hit the hamster wheel, burn out, stop compounding. They never prioritized longevity."
