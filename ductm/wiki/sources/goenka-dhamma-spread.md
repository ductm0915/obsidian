# May Dhamma Spread! — S.N. Goenka (Yangon 2012)

**Type:** Public talk (edited for publication)  
**Speaker:** S.N. Goenka  
**Date:** December 22, 2012  
**Venue:** Yangon, Myanmar  
**File:** `raw/articles/Vipassana/S.N Goenka/May Dhamma spread!.md`

---

## Summary

Goenka's final years talk reflecting on the global spread of Vipassana — why it's spreading, what makes it work, and his personal gratitude to Myanmar. Short (28 lines), but distills the organizational philosophy and universal claim of Vipassana.

---

## Key Claims

### Why Vipassana Is Spreading

**1. Not a religion**
- Buddha never taught religion — he taught Dhamma
- Vipassana is universal: "leaders and followers of every religion have joined courses and found Vipassana so useful"
- People find it good for everyone regardless of identity

**2. The dana model**
- Teaching, board, and lodging: **all free**
- "Once they benefit from the teaching, naturally they wish to offer support so that others can benefit in future"
- Not commercial, not converting — result-oriented and universal
- By 2012: 160+ centers, 1000+ teachers worldwide

**3. No conversion**
- "Whether Hindu, Buddhist, Christian, Muslim — it makes no difference"
- Practice Vipassana = practice Dhamma = free of religious rites, blind beliefs, dogmas, cults

### Goenka's Personal Story

- Born in Myanmar; considers it his real homeland regardless of passport
- Second birth: learning Vipassana from Sayagyi U Ba Khin — "made me a different person altogether"
- Gratitude to Myanmar for preserving technique "in its pristine purity" through the millennia
- Migraine cure + morphine dependence stopped **after one course** — but this was the by-product, not the main benefit
  > *"My mind became clearer; my life became more peaceful and harmonious. The cure of my disease was just a by-product."*

### The Universal Formula

> *"Vipassana is living a moral life. Which religion will oppose this? And to live a moral life, you must have a strong mind. Which religion will contradict this? And then you must have a pure mind. So sila, samadhi, panna — morality, concentration, purifying insight. Nobody has any objection to that."*

**Sila-Samadhi-Panna** as universal formula → see [[sila-samadhi-panna]]

## Notable Quotes

> *"Vipassana will make you a healthy person at the mental level. Your mind will be more calm and quiet. You will live a prosperous, harmonious life, enjoying good relations with members of your family and others. Vipassana is a way of life."*

> *"May more and more people benefit from Vipassana to live a happy and harmonious life, good for them and good for others!"*
