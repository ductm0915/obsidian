# Source Summary

Playbook focused on building a brand that compounds over time. Connects directly to [[branding-vs-advertising]] — branding creates deliberate associations that change buyer behavior at scale, while advertising is simply making people aware.

## Key Claims
- Brand is the sum of all associations people have with you/your business.
- Great branding amplifies every other marketing and sales activity.
- Branding compounds — unlike paid ads that stop when you stop paying.
