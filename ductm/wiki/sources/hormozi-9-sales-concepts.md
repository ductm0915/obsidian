# Source Summary

Hormozi distills the 9 things brilliant salespeople do differently, organized into 3 buckets from building multiple 9-figure sales teams and closing 4,000+ deals personally.

## The 3 Buckets
1. **Maximize Opportunities**: Total available hours, sell 7 days/week, speed-to-lead. Availability is the single greatest lever on throughput.
2. **Maximize Conversion Rate**: Master the [[aaa-method]], use reframing, handle objections without losing rapport.
3. **Do It Consistently For a Long Time**: [[volume-negates-luck]], maintain discipline and process adherence.

## Key Insight
- The guy who works the most hours is the one who sells the most deals — no exceptions observed across portfolio companies.
