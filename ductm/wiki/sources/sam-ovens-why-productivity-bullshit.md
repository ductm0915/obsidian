# Sam Ovens — Why Productivity Is Bullshit! The Secret Is To Do Less Not More

**Author:** [[sam-ovens]]  
**Type:** YouTube video (with live appearance by Rhett, Sam's live-in personal trainer)  
**Core theme:** "Productivity" as it's commonly practiced is a trap. Doing less and concentrating energy produces exponentially more output.

---

## Summary

Sam opens by mocking the productivity-industrial complex: to-do list apps, productivity books, seminars — all of which are themselves distractions from the work. His central metaphor:

**Energy as vector quantity.** If you have 9 units of energy and split them across 9 goals, you make 1 unit of progress on each. If you concentrate all 9 units on 1 goal, you make 9 units of progress. This is mathematics, not motivation. Michael Phelps' entire to-do list: wake up, get in pool.

**Practical elimination examples Sam uses personally:**

1. **Personal chef (Alyssa)** — 4 days/week, 3 hours/session, covers all meals for 6 days. No more: "what should I eat / where / how long will it take." Frees massive cognitive bandwidth.

2. **Live-in personal trainer (Rhett)** — at Sam's door at 6:50am. No more deciding when/whether to go to gym. Rhett also: plans meals, orders Whole Foods delivery, tracks health stats via Oura Ring, manages Sam's sleep/supplement routine.

3. **Phone discipline** — 4 apps, all notifications off, phone on airplane mode in a drawer during work. Change phone number every few months to eliminate accumulated contacts who can interrupt.

4. **Email checked every 3 days** — not reactively. "Nothing is so urgent that people need to reach me right now."

5. **Routine-as-infrastructure** — fixed wake time, gym, shower, smoothie, meditation, customer interview, then work. Every day. Sunday is the only variable day.

6. **One product, one business** — Consulting.com with essentially one product for 5–6 years before adding a second. Multiple businesses = the energy diagram scattered to the winds. "I see someone with multiple businesses and I know they're poor."

**Key sleep insight (from Rhett):** Sleep is the most impactful health variable — above diet and exercise. Skimping on sleep weakens the conscious mind (the "police officer" of your behavior). Sleep deprivation is exactly what cults and the military use to increase compliance. 8 hours minimum.

---

## Key Claims

1. **Doing more gets you less.** This is not philosophy — it's math. Energy is finite and vectors matter.

2. **The more money you make, the more you should delegate personal logistics.** At $10K/month: hire a chef. Below that: batch cook on Sundays. The cognitive cost of food decisions is always too high relative to the dollar cost of a chef.

3. **Social media is "the number one cancer" for getting things done.** Delete everything. 30 minutes per day max. Use only as producer, never as consumer.

4. **"If you don't have a plan, you will become a part of somebody else's."** Every notification, email, call is someone else's plan imposing on yours.

5. **One business, one product, until you have 50 employees and 5-6 years in** before considering adding a second product. Then only with excess capacity.
