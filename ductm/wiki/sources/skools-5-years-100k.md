# 5 Years Growing Skools to $100,000/month

**Author:** Unidentified Skool educator (early investor in Skool, hosted Hormozi Skool Games, founded Synthesizer community)  
**Type:** YouTube video transcript  
**Date ingested:** 2026-04-10

## Summary
First-person account of a 5-year journey building on the Skool platform, structured as 6 seasons. Key through-lines: focus over breadth, charging for high value, content as traffic engine, impact over income, and the power of partnerships. Speaker helped 100+ people reach $100K/month on Skool and 1,000+ reach $10K/month.

## The 6 Seasons

### Season 1 — The One Source Rule
Overwhelmed by contradictory advice from many online mentors, the speaker committed to **one source for 90 days** (Sam Ovens). Insight: learning online is like solving a jigsaw puzzle — adding a second puzzle's pieces to the first makes both unsolvable. Complete one puzzle fully before moving on.
- → [[one-source-rule]]

### Season 2 — High-Ticket Discovery
Moved from $7–$49 products to $1,000–$3,800 offers (breakup coaching, then productivity/procrastination). Discovery: to make $10K/month you need 10 customers at $1K vs. 200 customers at $50. "Problems have I solved?" + "Who can I reach?" = niche selection formula.

### Season 3 — Content as Traffic
Built YouTube channel to 600K subscribers. Went from ~$1K/month to $30K/month in 6 months and ultimately $100K/month from single YouTube-to-one-offer funnel. Formula: **product-market fit + traffic**. Notable: ended up working with Mr. Beast as a client.

### Season 4 — The Hollow Peak
Hit financial freedom at age 21 but felt hollow. Realized: impact matters as much as income. Started **Synthesizer community** — free community for educational content creators using content (not code) to reach financial freedom. Named from Naval Ravikant's concept of "two forms of modern wealth: content and code."

### Season 5 — Scaling Others on Skool
Helped Hamza grow from $20K to $200K/month; helped Reen Torres from 0 to 300K subscribers in 12 months. Worked with Hormozi's Skool to attract large creators. Hosted Hormozi Skool Games. Ran "The 100" challenge: average participant added $15,373/month in 90 days. Lesson: follow curiosity, get so good you can't be ignored.

### Season 6 — Partnership (Aiden)
Met Aiden through a $50 Skool community. Aiden consistently over-delivered → trusted him with more → Aiden went from $0 to $300K/month in under 12 months. Core lesson: **it's about who, not what**.

## Key Claims
- "The first rule of business is learn from people ahead of you."
- One funnel (YouTube → one product) can get to $100K/month.
- Financial freedom without purpose feels hollow; impact refuels motivation.
- Don't follow trends — follow curiosity and become category of one.
