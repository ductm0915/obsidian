## Core Teaching: Ba Cấp Độ Trí Tuệ

Day 3 discourse is the **philosophical keystone** of the course — Goenka explains why Vipassana is not religion, devotion, or intellectual exercise, but direct experiential wisdom.

### Framework: Tam Tuệ (Three Wisdoms)

| Pali Term | Vietnamese | Source | Limitation |
|-----------|------------|--------|------------|
| **Suta-maya-paññā** | Văn Tuệ — Trí tuệ thủ nhận | Nghe/đọc từ người khác | Có thể trở thành mù quáng (đức tin không kiểm chứng) |
| **Cinta-maya-paññā** | Tư Tuệ — Trí tuệ phân tích | Suy luận lý trí | Có thể trở thành tự mãn (ngụy biện để bảo vệ định kiến) |
| **Bhāvanā-maya-paññā** | Tu Tuệ — Trí tuệ thực chứng | Tự kinh nghiệm trực tiếp | **Đây là trí tuệ duy nhất đưa đến giải thoát** |

**Goenka's core argument:** The first two wisdoms are necessary but insufficient. Only *bhāvanā-maya-paññā* — wisdom born from direct personal experience — breaks the cycle of suffering. The Buddha did not discover a new doctrine; he rediscovered the *methodology* of direct experiential inquiry.

## Key Pedagogical Framework: The Medicine Metaphor

Goenka uses a **doctor-patient metaphor** (appearing also in Day 1) with a new twist:

> "Bệnh nhân cầm toa thuốc rồi đi 108 vòng quanh bàn thờ ảnh bác sĩ... Hay tụng niệm toa thuốc sáng một viên trưa một viên... Làm như thế sẽ chữa được bệnh không?"

The three responses to the prescription mirror the three wisdoms:
1. **Blind faith** (Suta-maya) → treating the prescription as scripture
2. **Intellectual analysis** (Cinta-maya) → debating the prescription with others
3. **Taking the medicine** (Bhāvanā-maya) → the only thing that cures

**Application to leadership:** Knowing the right strategy intellectually (Suta) and analyzing it logically (Cinta) are insufficient. Only *implementing and experiencing* the results builds real wisdom.

## The Blind Man Parable: On Limits of Second-Hand Knowledge

Goenka tells the story of a blind man trying to understand whiteness through metaphor:
- "Chắn như con vịt trắng" → mềm (confuses softeness with colour)
- "Cong quẹo" (crooked) → the blind man's version of "white"

**Core insight:** Without direct experience (*bhāvanā*), even intelligent people create systematically distorted mental models. The map is never the territory.

## The Three Characteristics: Anicca-Dukkha-Anattā at Experiential Level

Day 3 introduces the three marks of existence not as philosophy but as **experiential milestones**:

### 1. Anicca (Vô Thường — Impermanence)
- Not just intellectual acceptance of change
- Direct experience: the body's atomic structure arising and passing billions of times per finger-snap
- Goenka quotes modern physics (Berkeley professor): "Each subatomic particle arises and passes 10²² times per second"
- Also the Buddha's realization: *"Sabbo pajjalito loko"* — the entire universe is vibration

### 2. Dukkha (Khổ — Suffering)
- Even pleasant sensations are dukkha if you cling to them
- The "morning bliss / afternoon agony" trap: meditators who experience jhāna states in the morning and become attached → suffer when states change by afternoon
- **Key distinction:** Suffering ≠ only pain. Clinging to pleasure = also suffering.

### 3. Anattā (Vô Ngã — No-Self)
- "Cái tôi là cái gì?" — Dissecting the self: hair, teeth, nails → each disgusting when separated from the whole
- At ultimate level: only kalāpas (subatomic particles) arising and passing — no permanent self to find
- Goenka: "Chúng ta phải dùng chữ 'tôi' vì theo lề thói thông thường — thật ra chẳng có gì là cái tôi"

## The Bubble-and-Wave Model of Reality

**Framework:** "Toàn thể vũ trụ chỉ là những làn sóng li ti sinh diệt liên tục"

| Object | Apparent Truth | Ultimate Truth |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| Candle flame | Same flame burning | New flame each millisecond |
| River | Same river | Constantly replaced water |
| Body at 20 vs 80 | Same person | Continuous atomic regeneration |
| "I" | Stable self | Stream of kalāpas |

This model connects to the [[anicca-impermanence]] concept page and the scientific finding that matter at the quantum level is pure vibration.

## Day 3 Closing Instructions

Goenka ends with the **transition announcement**: Starting Day 4, Anapana gives way to Vipassana proper. Day 3 has been the preparation.

Key instruction: "Đừng trông mong thấy hàng tỉ vi tử ngay khi bắt đầu — ban đầu quý vị sẽ thấy cảm giác nặng nề. Từ từ chúng sẽ mất đi."

Attitude required: *Acceptance of what is* — không tưởng tượng, không tụng niệm, không quán tưởng. Chỉ thực tại.
