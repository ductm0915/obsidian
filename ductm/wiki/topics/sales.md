# Sales

## Overview
Sales is the process of helping individuals or organizations make a buying decision. Rather than an act of pressure, modern frameworks define sales as a natural extension of marketing—a personalized, contextual education required to bridge the gap of trust and belief.

## Core Concepts

### Frameworks
- [[closer-framework]]: C/L/O/S/E/R — the step-by-step sales script structure for any ticket size.
- [[sell-the-vacation]]: Sell the destination (outcome), never the plane flight (process/features).
- [[diagnostic-sales-process]]: Current/Desired/Obstacle → price tied directly to the goal gap.
- [[aaa-method]]: Objection handling — 5 types (Time, Money, Fit, Authority, Avoidance) mapped to 3 distortions.
- [[blame-distortions]]: All objections trace to three CBT distortions (Circumstances, Others, Self).
- [[conviction-framework]]: Words hit logic; tone hits emotion. Conviction corrects tone automatically.

- [[grand-slam-offer]]: Engineering offers so good people feel stupid saying no.
- [[value-equation]]: Value = (Dream Outcome × Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort).

## Selling AI Services

New angle from [[claude-code-build-sell-course]]:

- [[doctor-vs-pharmacist]]: Diagnose the real business problem before proposing a solution — don't just build what they ask for.
- **Value-based pricing** for AI services: Price reflects value delivered (hours saved, errors eliminated, revenue generated), not time spent building. Ties directly to [[value-equation]].
- Lộ trình: Freelancer → Consultant → Trusted Partner. $3K build → $50K/year relationship.
- Lead with outcomes: "Tôi tiết kiệm X giờ/tháng" > "Tôi build agentic workflows."
