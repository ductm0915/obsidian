# Credibility Bank

The **Credibility Bank** is a strategic inventory of an individual's achievements, successes, proven results, and lessons learned from mistakes. It serves as the foundational "proof of work" that justifies why an audience should listen to an expert.

## Expert vs. Student Implementation
- **For Experts**: The bank is filled with "W's" (wins/successes). The more impressive the bank, the faster the brand growth, as it builds immediate buy-in.
- **For Students**: Rather than a Credibility Bank, students build an **Interest Bank**—a running list of what they are testing, learning, and documenting. Their "right" to speak comes from being a proxy/guinea pig for the audience.

## Tactical Utility
The items in the Credibility Bank are the raw materials for content. They move the brand away from "sharing tactics" (which anyone can do) toward "teaching principles" derived from real-world outcomes.
