# Brand Journey Framework

The **Brand Journey Framework** is [[caleb-ralston]]'s foundational 4-question framework that reverse-engineers from a desired outcome to actionable steps for today. It answers "why am I building this brand?" and provides the roadmap.

## The 4 Questions

1. **What is my desired outcome?** — Start with the end in mind. What do I want to have happen?
2. **What must I be known for?** — In order for that outcome to happen, what reputation do I need?
3. **What must I do?** — We become known through actions, not just words. What actions must I take?
4. **What must I learn?** — Starting from today: what skills, knowledge, or capabilities do I need to develop?

## Example
- **Desired outcome**: Speak on stages to help young women grow their businesses.
- **Known for**: Being a woman who has built and helped others build successful businesses.
- **Must do**: Build a business + help other women build theirs.
- **Must learn**: HR, marketing, recruiting, sales, product development.

## Strategic Use
- If building a personal brand doesn't accelerate your desired outcome, don't build one. (Gary Vaynerchuk's advice to Caleb.)
- Every content decision should map back to one of your desired outcomes. If it doesn't serve goal #1 or #2, don't publish it.
- The framework provides clarity that prevents "wandering aimlessly" — the Alice in Wonderland problem.
