# Value Equation

Alex Hormozi's formula for quantifying perceived value of any offer:

> **Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)**

## The Four Levers
1. **Dream Outcome** (↑): What the customer ultimately wants. The bigger and more desirable, the more valuable.
2. **Perceived Likelihood of Achievement** (↑): How confident they are it will work for them. Proof, testimonials, guarantees increase this.
3. **Time Delay** (↓): How long until they get the result. Faster = more valuable.
4. **Effort & Sacrifice** (↓): How much work/pain they have to endure. Less effort = more valuable.

## Application
- When creating a [[grand-slam-offer]], maximize the numerator (dream outcome + likelihood) and minimize the denominator (time + effort).
- This equation explains why "done-for-you" services command premium prices — they minimize effort and time delay simultaneously.
