# Interest Media

A shift from traditional "Social Media" algorithms to algorithms based entirely on consumer interest.

Platforms are moving towards serving content based purely on what holds a specific user's attention (their interests), regardless of who they follow or are socially connected to. Content scales based on topic resonance rather than existing audience size. 

*Related implication:* Getting views from unrelated viral content (e.g. a public prank) holds no value for a business because the audience is interested in "comedy" not the business's core offering. Attention must be captured within the target interest graph.
