# Sales Multipliers

## Definition
Sales multipliers are systemic optimizations and behaviors that exponentially increase the volume and conversion of a sales operation without necessarily increasing headcount. 

## Key Multipliers
According to [[alex-hormozi]]:
1. **Speed to Lead**: Responding in under 1 minute.
2. **7 Days a Week Availability**: Running operations through weekends capitalizes on high buyer activity and intent.
3. **15-Minute Scheduling Blocks**: Increasing appointment density over the traditional 30 or 60-minute blocks creates artificial urgency and fits more calls into a day.

## Application
These multipliers hinge on extreme operational efficiency. The premise is that small logistical edges compound massively over hundreds of leads.
