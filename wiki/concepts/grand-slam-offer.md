# Grand Slam Offer

An offer so good people feel stupid saying no. Coined by [[alex-hormozi]] in $100M Offers.

## Core Properties
- Solves a massive, painful problem for a specific audience.
- Stacks value through bonuses, guarantees, scarcity, and urgency far beyond the price.
- Creates a "category of one" — no direct comparison exists, making price objections irrelevant.
- Built using the [[value-equation]]: maximize dream outcome and perceived likelihood, minimize time delay and effort.

## Construction Steps
1. Identify the dream outcome your ideal customer wants.
2. List every problem/obstacle between them and that outcome.
3. Turn each obstacle into a solution (product, service, bonus).
4. Stack all solutions into one irresistible bundle.
5. Add guarantees to eliminate risk.
6. Add scarcity/urgency to drive action.
