# The 3 Stages of Your Personal Brand (Full Course)

[[caleb-ralston]] maps the personal brand journey into three stages with distinct strategies for each, arguing that most creators stay stuck because they use their starting strategy forever.

## Key Claims and Arguments

### Blueprint Exercises (Apply to All Stages)
1. **Brand Journey Framework**: 4 questions from desired outcome → today.
2. **Brand Associations**: Write 4 — 2 things you're FOR, 2 you're AGAINST.
3. **Brand Positioning**: What belief do you hold that is fundamentally different than your peers?
4. **Medium Selection**: Video, Audio, Written, or Photo/Graphic — pick what's most natural.

### Stage 1: Building the Foundation (0–100K)
- ~90% of creators are in this stage. 90% of those are under 20K followers.
- **Primary Focus**: Build the habit of creating at a sustainable pace. Prove to your audience you're worth following.
- **Volume for Learnings**: Use the [[accordion-method]] — expand (high volume) to learn what resonates, then contract to optimize.
- **Niche-Wide Content**: Stay within your domain but vary width. Deep (how to lead a media team) vs. wide (struggling with content). Both stay niche-relevant.
- **Pitfalls**: Building around a niche you hate, trying to monetize too early, posting only when motivated.
- **Nike × Jordan Metaphor**: If Jordan wore Nikes one game, no association. Repetition of the same core message = branding.

### Stage 2: Scaling the Brand (100K–500K)
- Shift from volume-for-learnings to optimizing what works.
- Contract the accordion: same effort → fewer, better pieces.
- Begin leveraging the [[eye-of-sauron-method]] for multi-platform innovation rotation.

### Stage 3: Sustaining & Evolving (500K+)
- Innovate continuously; audience fatigue is real. Keep rotating the Eye of Sauron.
- The brand's biggest risk is stagnation from doing what always worked.

### Contrarian Belief Deep Dive
- **3 Levers to Stand Out**: (1) Contrarian belief = 80% lever, (2) Format/style, (3) Personality.
- **Contrarian vs. Controversial**: Contrarian = genuine belief. Controversial = designed to trigger. "Nobody with a strong brand wants to pair with a loose cannon."
- **Examples**: Patagonia (environment > profit), Netflix (people don't want to leave home), Gary Vee (social > legacy media), Cody Sanchez (boring businesses > tech startups).
- **Test and Double Down**: Start with multiple contrarian takes. Observe which catches fire. Double down.
- Caleb started with 3 takes: (1) optimize trust > virality, (2) sustainable system > omnipresence, (3) psychological safety > fear-based leadership. Trust optimization caught fire.

## Notable Quotes

> "Your personal brand is in one of these stages. The majority of you stay stuck because you're using the strategy that you started with."

> "We use quantity to inform what quality is. You post more to find out what your audience is vibing with and then you do more of that."

> "If you build a controversial brand, most people who build a controversial brand are kind of known as a loose cannon."
