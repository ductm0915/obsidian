  * [[Set up]]
    * Bài tham khảo
      * 🔥 Write your About Page description
        * 🚀 Create and upload a Cover image from Canva - 1400x790
        * 🚀 Create and upload an Icon image from Canva - 128x128
        * 🚀 Create 3-5 other graphics highlighting key benefits of your group
        * 🚀 Create an intro Video about what your group does for people - focus on the main thing they will accomplish over the next 30 days!
      * 🤼 Write your Intro / Start Here post in your community
        * ⭐⭐ Copy the link to you Intro / Start Here post
        * Go to Settings
      * 💲 Determine and set your Price if Paid, otherwise leave it as Free
      * 😲 Set up your Categories - keep it simple - less is better to start
      * 🔑 Turn on key Plugins
        * 🔑🧲 Membership Questions - can have up to three questions
        * 🔑🧲 Auto DM for new members - keep it fun and link to your Intro / Start Here Post
        * 🔑🧲 Unlock Chat at Level 2 - turn this on to help keep Spam down
      * 🤼 Gamification - this is where you customized the levels for the Points / Leaderboard.
      * I suggest using AI to help with writing your Level Descriptions (I will post about this)
      * 🤖 Add your Skool Group Links
      * 🚩 Start Here Link
      * 🚩 Link to your website and/or key offer
      * 🚩 Link to your affiliate Skool Start Group
      * 🤠 Once your group is set up and launched, edit your personal profile with a link to your Group. 🤠
      * Let me know any steps I missed so I can add them. 💜💥
