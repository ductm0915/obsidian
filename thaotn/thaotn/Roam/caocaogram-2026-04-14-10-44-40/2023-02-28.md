    * # Lay the Right Foundation for Your Employees
      * The first days in the life of an employee will set the tone for their future. Today we will talk about laying the right foundation for the employees.
      * ### Key Learning Points
        * A new employee must feel relaxed and welcome on their first day at the job because their experience in the beginning will set the tone for their future.
        * Create procedures for induction and training to ensure continuity of good quality.
        * Focus on the exercise in the first week or two of their training to set the tone for their work ethic for the future of your company.
      * Những ngày đầu tiên rất quan trọng
      * Khi cảm xúc tốt thì mọi thứ sẽ tốt theo

        * Introduction Tour: nơi làm việc bao gồm những gì, cách giao tiếp: khiến họ cảm thấy bình tâm, thoải mái,...
