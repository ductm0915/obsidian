      * If I could give you a device that spits out $2, $3, or even $6 for every $1 you insert into this magical machine, would you like to have it?

      * But there’s a problem.
      * Which is this.
      * Our impact will always be limited, if we don’t have the necessary profits to sustain business growth and to reach more people with our message, products and services.
      * So then, it stands to reason that we need to get our magical money machine to work.
      * Because when we have the financial resources, we can make all the impact we desire and make the world a better place.
      * So back to that magical money machine...
      * You might not know this, but you have access to this magical money machine.
      * Today I’m going to hand you the instructions to get everything dialled in just right, so you can grow your business and impact to whatever level you desire.
      * That magical money machine is paid advertising and one of the greatest skills in business is when you can turn paid advertising into profit.
      * And I know of no better methodology than the ASK Method to get this dialled in just right.
      * Once you have your Funnel ready, you can use paid traffic to accelerate your results.
      * I’m going to give you the sheet we use whenever we launch a quiz funnel so we can track all the stats and make the necessary tweaks to get that machine to spit out $2, $3 or even $6 for every $1 we insert.
      * As a business owner it’s essential that you have the right data at hand, so you can make wise and prosperous financial decisions.
      * This sheet will specifically deal with front end sales of a quiz funnel and once you get it dialled in just right, you can get on the fast lane to your impact and legacy.
      * Before we get into the nitty gritty here’s a quick pep talk…
      * For some of you this might look just a little intimidating, but we're talking about a magical money machine here, so it's definitely worth figuring it out right?
        * And remember, that everything is figure-outable!
      * Secondly you don't have to be the person doing it, but it helps to understand the high level mechanics of this magical money machine.
      * So with that said, let’s dive in.
      * The sheet contains multiple date ranges which is very handy when it comes to viewing the data.
      * You can see the weekly, monthly, multiple months or even the lifetime stats of your campaign.
      * The ROI Column has conditional formatting, so any negative returns will have red cell fills, whereas positive returns will have green cell fills.
      * The blue columns are where you need to enter your data.
      * The yellow columns will do all the calculations.
      * The attached sheet has been populated with some sample data so you can see it in action.
      * Plus there’s a blank sheet too that you can use for your campaigns.
      * Instructions
        * Enter your Ad Spend
          * We get this from Facebook or any other traffic source by setting a date range and getting the total ad spend of the campaign for that duration.
        * Enter Clicks
          * We get this from Facebook by setting a date range and getting the total clicks generated from the campaign for that duration.
        * Enter Quiz Completions
          * We get this using the Quiz Optin Tag and date range in our CRM (Infusionsoft, Active Campaign etc). You can also get this from Bucket.io
        * Enter the number of sales generated
          * We get this using the Customer Tag and date range in our CRM.
        * Enter the product price.
        * Enter the number of upsell sales generated
          * We get this using the Customer Upsell Tag and date range in our CRM.
        *  Enter The Upsell Price.
      * Once you’ve entered all the data above, you will get the following stats automatically calculated for you.
      * = Total ROI %
        * The total return on investment percentage. For example if you spent $100 in ads and made $200 in revenue then you would have made a 100% return on investment.
        * The calculation for ROI is (Total Earnings - Investment) / Investment * 100
      * = The Magical DIDO Stat - “Dollar In, Dollar Out”.
        * This shows how many dollars you make/lose for every dollar spent on ads.
        * The calculation is your revenue generated divided by ad spend.
      * = Total Revenue Generated
        * The total amount of money generated from the campaign.
      * = Total Profit Generated
        * This doesn’t take into account any COGS. (Costs of Goods Sold.)
        * For most of us that will be zero anyway since we’re selling digital info products.
        * The calculation for this is your revenue - your ad spend.
      * = CPC - Cost Per Click.
        * The amount you’re being charged to get a click.
        * The calculation for CPC is your ad spend divided by the total number of clicks.
      * = EPC - Earnings Per Click.
        * The amount you make from every click you buy.
        * The calculation for EPC is your revenue generated divided by clicks.
      * = CPL - Cost Per Lead.
        * How much you pay for a new lead, or quiz completion in this example.
        * The calculation for CPL is your ad spend divided by your quiz completions.
      * = EPL - Earnings Per Lead.
        * How much you make from every new lead.
        * The calculation for EPL is your revenue generated divided by your leads/quiz completions.
      * = CPA - Cost Per Acquisition.
        * How much it costs to acquire/buy a new customer.
        * The calculation for CPA is your ad spend divided by the number of sales.
      * = ACV - Average Cart Value (What a new customer is worth to you.)
        * This only deals with the front end sale and doesn’t take into account backend or subscription prices.
        * To properly capture all that and calculate your LTV (LifeTime Value) of a customer extends beyond the reach of this sheet.
        * The calculation for ACV is your revenue divided by the number of sales.
      * = Lead Capture Conversion Rate.
        * The percentage of people that opt in after taking the quiz.
        * You should aim for 50% or higher.
        * The calculation for this is your quiz completions divided by clicks.
      * = Sales Conversion Rate.
        * The percentage of leads that become a customer.
        * The calculation for this is the number of sales divided by quiz completions.
      * = Upsell Conversion Rate.
        * The percentage of customers that buy your upsell.
        * An upsell can make a massive difference in your overall stats since it increases your average cart value and makes it easier to turn a profit from your ad spend.
        * The calculation for this is your number of upsells divided by number of sales.
