    * Make customer buy from you
      * The easier it is (emotionally, logically, and logistically) it is for people to buy, the more likely it is that they will.
      * Inception marketing is a layered process so always think ahead about your customer's next step and make sure that it is as easy as possible for them to buy.
      * A well constructed and well delivered Inceptive Story will make people curious about buying already; maybe even ready to buy. So your job is to make sure that it is easy as possible for people to buy. To do so, it is important to:
        * Give them proof, bằng chứng
        * Reverse the risk, rủi ro
        * Make it very easy and clear how to buy right now, rõ ràng cách mua
        * Give them the incentive to act now, đưa khích lệ để mua
        * Walk them through the process, step by step, of how to buy and what happens after they buy.
