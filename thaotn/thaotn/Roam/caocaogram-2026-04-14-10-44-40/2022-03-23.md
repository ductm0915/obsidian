        * Bộ câu hỏi: SCORE
        * S: Systom - these are the immediate signs that tell you there is a problem.
        * C: Cause - which may be the antecedent conditions that gave rise to the symptoms, the intentions behind behaviours giving rise to the problem, or current constraints.
        * O: Outcome - your desired result or goal, where you want to get to.
        * R: Resource - the qualities, capabilities, reserves and help that you can bring to bear on solving the problem. These can be past, present or future.
        * E - Effect - the longer term, systemic and higher level results of the outcome.
