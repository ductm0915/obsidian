              * Bài 0: Structure Your Course
                * **Key Learning Points**
                  * Being a great presenter is not just a talent, but a skill you can learn.
                  * Creating a course inspired by your knowledge and experiences benefits the world.
                  * Most of your fears are just an illusion. If they happen to manifest, you can always overcome them.
                * **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Think about what type of course you want to create.
                  * What is your end goal in building your course?
                  * Ponder over how your course can solve a problem.
              * Bài 0: The Models of Online Learning
                * **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. There are two main types of courses: MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) and CBCs (Cohort Based Courses - mở theo mỗi ngày một chút).
                  * 2. The sign-up rate for MOOCs is high, but the completion rate on average is only around 5%.
                  * 3. There are three main advantages of the CBCs:
                    * Based on cohorts with concrete start dates.
                    * Contain bite-size and digestible pieces of information.
                    * Offer the option to interact with the community.
            * Part 1: Unleash Your Uniqueness
              * Bài 1: How Course Creation Can Change Your Life
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Compelling visions keep us pushing forward, and are essential when building a course.
                  * 2. The benefits of having an online course are building a legacy, earning money, and creating a strong brand.
                  * 3. There are two main ways to leverage online courses:
                    * Offer free access to your premium program.
                    * Showcase your content online.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Think about the next 12 months and where you want to be in a year.
                  *  Stick the paper with the list of benefits somewhere visible to see every day.
              * Bài 2: Why Your Fears Are an Illusion
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. If you allow your fears to dwarf you, you will never find the courage to do what you want to do.
                  * 2. What we fear can be prevented, but if they happen to manifest, they can always be overcome.
                  * 3. The most common fears holding us back are:
                    * The belief, ‘I’m not good enough.’
                    * The course won’t live up to expectations.
                    * Nobody will buy the course.
                    * Others won’t like the content.
                    * The fear of bad reviews.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Think about your fears and write them down.
                  * Ask yourself what you can do to prevent the worst-case scenario from happening.
              * Bài 3: Having the Courage to Create
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. A powerful identity is the foundation for how you see yourself. It's important to build one that serves you.
                  * 2. When you compare yourself to other people, it's easy to fall into the trap of underestimating yourself and overestimating other people.
                  * 3. You are better than you think you are. When you work on your identity, make sure it’s an empowering one.****
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * 1. Imagine being an actor and playing out different roles.
                  * 2. Complete the ‘‘Why I’m Great” List Exercise:
                    * Think about and write down all the positive things pertaining to you.
                    * Think about and write down all the things you take for granted that seemed impossible in the past.
              * Bài 4: Discover and Infuse Your Values Into Your Brand
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * There are three main personal brand and style concepts: brand frequency, your personal style, and brand photography.
                  * To become iconic, you have to have a narrow brand frequency. Singular brand frequency is super important.
                  * You can incorporate what you love with your style, but you want to ensure that you have a style.
                  * Good photography uplifts the sales up to 20%, speaks to people subconsciously, and builds credibility.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Write down your brand frequency.
                  * Find something to wear that represents your frequency.
                  * Invest in good photography.
              * Bài 5: Get Clear on Your Filming Options
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. When filming a course, you can do it all by yourself, pay professionals, or use a value exchange filming option.
                  * 2. You can use a regular phone to make decent videos, but make sure you use a good quality microphone.
                  * 3. When filming a course:
                    * Shoot from the waist up.
                    * Show your palms.
                    * Have minimum light changes throughout the shoot.
                  * 4. When you pay for filming and editing or use the value exchange filming option, make sure that both parties understand the deal.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Think about all the filming options presented in this lesson.
                  * Pick the filming option that suits you best and take the first step to make it happen.

              * Bài 6: Overcome Toxic Perfectionism
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Toxic perfectionism may prevent you from taking any action. It’s better to have a smaller, shorter course completed than getting stuck with massive aspirations that may lead nowhere.
                  * 2. Start small by creating a minimum viable product that will give you valuable feedback from your audience so you can improve the content and fill the gaps if needed.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Start thinking about the minimum viable product that you can build.
                  * Define and write down the specifics of the duration of your course.
              * Bài 7: Summary Of The First Part
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * The benefits of having an online course are building a legacy and creating a strong brand.
                  * Fears can be prevented, but if what we’re worried about ends up happening, there will always be a solution.
                  * It’s important to build a powerful identity that serves you.
                  * The three main personal brand concepts are brand frequency, personal style, and photography.
                  *  It’s possible to shoot on any budget and get a decent result.
                  * Avoid toxic perfectionism to keep you going.

            * Part 2: Design Your Course
              * Bài 8: What to Teach and How to Position Yourself
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Trying to serve everyone results in serving no one. If you want to make it big, pick a niche for your course.
                  * 2. Combine your course topic that matches your unique experience and style. This will help you stand out from other course creators.
                  * 3. Define your avatar figure to determine who is your target audience and how you can solve their challenges and problems.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Think about the ‘impossible’ challenges that you managed to overcome.
                  * Come up with your topic that has a unique angle and begin drafting it.
                  * Find an avatar and describe it in great detail.
                  * Share your avatar with the Mindvalley Tribe.
              * Bài 9: Shape Your Identity for a Boost in Confidence
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Speak naturally on camera as if you’re having a conversation with a friend.
                  * 2. The two pillars of consciousness engineering consist of installing a new model of reality in someone and teaching them a new system for living.
                  * 3. Shape your own identity and overcome resistance because of your new identity.
                  * 4. Stack a new habit on top of an existing one to set people up for success.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Come up with your identity and write it down.
                  * Practice a habit stacking technique when creating your course.
              * Bài 10: Design Courses That Create Shifts in Your Student
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * The Six Shifts exercise helps create a vision of how you want to transform your students when creating a course.
                    * The mindset shift provides a new and extraordinary way of looking at the world.
                    * The habit shift helps to change people’s habits.
                    * A lifestyle shift goes beyond a habit shift.
                    * The show-up shift is how you show up in the world and how people see you.
                    * The toolkit upgrade is a shift that the student can use as a tool.
                    * The special power shift can be used for a spiritual course.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Think about the Six Shifts that you can provide your student with.
                  * Write down one shift in each category.
              * Bài 11: Understanding a Winning Course Structure
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. The landing page is a web page where people land before buying your course.
                  * 2. The main landing page elements are the description, curriculum, reviews, the money-back guarantee, and biography.
                  * 3. The crucial videos every course should have are:
                    * Promotional video
                    * Introduction video
                    * Main content pieces
                    * Thank you video
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Look at the structure of some of your favorite courses.
                  * Have a mini case study of the structure elements learned in today’s lesson.
              * Bài 12: The Step-By-Step Content Creation Process
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Use reverse engineering when creating your curriculum. Start with the end goal in mind and identify where you want to go.
                  * 2. Make the curriculum easy, digestible, and clear for the students. Bring the content pieces under sections and combine them into lessons.
                  * 3. Use the Post-it Notes strategy to get a bird’s-eye view of your curriculum for efficient re-arrangement.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Ask yourself what the objectives of your customer avatar are.
                  * Write down as many content pieces as possible.
                  * Analyze all the content pieces and combine them into lessons.
                  * Create draft titles of the potential lessons.
                  * Identify the main idea clusters.
                  * Write down the draft section titles on separate pieces of paper.
                  * Arrange the papers and Post-it Notes in order.
              * Bài 13: How to Create Captivating Content That People Love
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Create hook points and open loops to captivate the student. Use the ABCS technique to highlight automation, benefit, create curiosity and specificity in your content.
                  * 2. Storytelling is a powerful way to engage the audience in an emotional way. Sharing a story propels your audience to learn and connect with you.
                  * 3. Using controversial statements, guest speakers, and case studies will spice up your content and make it more attractive to the student.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Apply the ABCS technique to the content you’re currently working on.
                  * Write down three stories that happened to you today.

              * Bài 14: Understand the 5 Rules of Human Transformation
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Learning is to take on new facts and new data. Transformation is to take on a new meaning schema of the world.
                  * 2. The Five Praxis of Transformation are:
                    * Critical reflection
                    * Critical study
                    * Social discourse
                    * Rate of application
                    * Altered states
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Think about The Five Praxis and write down at least three of them.
                  * Share your ideas with the Mindvalley Tribe (optional).

            * Part 3: Produce Your Course
              * Bài 16: Creating Magnetic Bonuses
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Bonuses are like extra gifts that can improve the quality of your score as well as accelerate sales.
                  * 2. A step ladder bonus is a bonus designed to make your program reachable to people.
                  * 3. A continuous bonus keeps you in the mind of the customer and brings them back.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Think about the bonuses you want to create.
                  * Test the first one out.
              * Bài 17: Prepare Your Written Content for On-Camera Delivery
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Maintain constant eye contact with your viewers.
                  * 2. Each lesson and video can be divided into separate content pieces and filmed individually.
                  * 3. When designing content pieces:
                    * Write down the video title.
                    * Figure out the objectives.
                    * Speak the objectives out loud.
                    * Create a list of bullet points.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * 1. Think about the objectives of your lesson.
                  * 2. Separate your videos into content pieces and write down the bullet points.
              * Bài 18: Presenting Your Course With Confidence and Charisma
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. When people buy online courses, they want to be guided in a compelling and entertaining way.
                  * 2. Strategy of Building Charisma:
                    * Tell a story and exert emotion.
                    * Address your audience directly.
                    * Add extra energy.
                    * Address the potential objections.
                    * Use positive body language.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Make a 30-second video on your phone.
                  * Make another video and pay attention to your body language.
              * Bài 19: Let’s Get Your "Pajama" Draft Filmed Today!
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. The pajama draft is filming your entire content from the beginning till the end, dressed up in something ridiculous.
                  * 2. The idea behind the pajama draft is to present your entire course from the beginning till the end with zero pressure.
                  * 3. Pajama Draft Steps:
                    * Set up your camera or phone on a tripod.
                    * Press record and start presenting.
                    * If you make a mistake, keep going.
                    * Have fun with it.
                    * Delete the videos.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Create your pajama draft.
                  * Optional: Share it with your Tribe.
              * Bài 20: Create Your Promotional Trailer Video
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. The trailer is the most important video of any course. If people like what they see, only then will they buy the course.
                  * 2. Trailer ingredients contain:
                    * The hook point
                    * Vulnerability combined with credibility
                    * Listing clear objectives
                    * Uniqueness and potential objections
                    * CTA and money-back guarantee
                    * Usage of b-rolls
                    * Scarcity
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Write down what you think worked well in Jimmy’s trailer and what could be improved.
                  * Think about the trailer ingredients and work on your draft.
                  * Optional: Share with your Mindvalley Tribe.
              * Bài 21: Let’s Get Your Course Filmed
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Being prepared for the shoot gives you the confidence to deliver in front of the camera.
                  * 2. Filming Tips:
                    * Have multiple filming sessions
                    * Separate content pieces
                    * Clap for a new take
                    * Create verbal notes
                    * Avoid perfectionism
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Review the techniques you’ve learned so far.
                  * Shoot the actual course.

            * Part 4: Publish and Market Your Course
              * Bài 23: Course Publishing Simplified
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Publishing platforms:
                    * Elite platforms
                    * Platforms with a marketplace
                    * Platforms without a marketplace
                  * 2. No course platform is inherently better than the other. You can use multiple platforms.
                  * 3. Platforms without a marketplace may be best suited for high-value courses.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Think about the different platforms.
                  * Write down the pros and cons of each platform.
              * Bài 24: Learn How to Create a Landing Page
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Udemy is an easy and straightforward platform for creating your first landing page.
                  * 2. Invest some time in testing out various landing page options before publishing your course.
                  * 3. Ask your friends to rate your course to make your landing page attractive to potential customers.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Create a draft landing page for your course.
                  * Play around with different settings to see what works best.
              * Bài 25: Rules of a Successful Course Launch
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. It’s important to work on your course launch even before the actual launch date to create hype.
                  * 2. Creating a snowball effect can be done by utilizing your inner circle and building social proof.
                  * 3. The Launch Strategies:
                    * Reach out to your inner circle.
                    * Build a spreadsheet.
                    * Inform your contacts before the launch.
                    * Send out the coupon codes.
                * ### **Your Actions for Today**
                  * Create a spreadsheet and write down your inner circle.
                  * Share the course with your inner circle.
                  * Optional: Share your course with the Mindvalley Tribe and ask for their feedback.
              * Bài 26: The Importance of Continuous Marketing
                * ### **Key Learning Points**
                  * 1. Continuous marketing means creating a lead capture ecosystem through which you can communicate with people.
                  * 2. The three main marketing tools are:
                    * Email funnels
                    * Youtube videos
                    * Instagram posts
                  * 3. Choose between emails and Youtube, but Instagram is a must.
