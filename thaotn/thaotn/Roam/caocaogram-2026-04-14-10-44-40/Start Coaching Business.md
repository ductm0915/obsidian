          * Each daily lesson consists of a 10-15 minute video and Actions Steps.

            * This is how you impact your clients and create transformation in their lives. It includes skills, strategies, techniques, systems and the innate intelligence that you bring.
            * You will learn:
              * The 10 principles of extraordinary coaching.
              * How to build strong boundaries that will manage all your relationships.

        * **FAQ**
          * I'm not ready to share certain parts of my life yet, what can I do?
            * You know how as coaches we need to do the work ourselves. The more we work on ourselves and do the deep work, the more comfortable we get with our story. There are certain things you'll probably never share. And others that you'll start sharing little by little.
          * I don't want to step on anyone's toes when I share my story, how can I avoid that?
            * Most times the story will only involve you and what it requires is your vulnerability. However when your story touches on other people (say your family for example) and you'd like to make sure that it's ok for you to share that story, then first share the story with them. Let them know that they are part of your story and how.
          * How do I know if my story lands well and how can I improve it?
            * That's what we have our community for. Share your story in a safe space like our community first. Get feedback and tell it again. Practice and refine it until you're happy with it.
          * I don't know all 4 stories yet, what do I do?
            * That's ok. They will reveal themselves with time. Start with 1 story and share that one.

          * First, you have to consider: Your goals, your preferred style of working with people, your client's goals.
          * Elements of the DSP framework: 1. Problem, 2. Desire, 3. Outcome, 4. Package, 5. Price, 6. Action Price
        * **More tips about packages:**
          * The formula is to come up with a ballpark but you can charge what feels best for you.
          * Your packages will change over time as you deliver them.
          * Listen what is working best for your clients and implement their preference.
          * Do not come from a place of lack (there are not enough clients)
          * Don’t discount. You are already giving them a price that feels good to you.
          * Don’t tweak a package to fit a client’s needs, you only offer a different one if it doesn’t fit for that person. If a lot of people ask you to tweak, then consider.
