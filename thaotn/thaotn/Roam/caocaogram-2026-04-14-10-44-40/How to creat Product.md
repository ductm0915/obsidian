  * 1. Format & Structure
    * **Step 1: Debrief the Original Course, Past Content**
      * Did a 360 review
        * Customer
        * Internal team
        * Outside consultants 
      * What was great
        * Original course was great
        * Lots of customers got results but Ryan knew it could be better
      * What review revealed: What could be better
        * Not enough step-by-step
        * Too much content (over whelm!)
          * Over teach, Over whelm
            * People try to put everything into one course
        * Scope (Bức tranh tổng quan / Bước nhìn lớn) wasn't as clear as it could be
          * Mantra (Châm ngôn): Less but better, less but more
        * Inconsistent use of terms, process, trainers
          * Not document so that not inconsistent when teaching though many  people
        * No great tools to help with implementation
    * **Step 2: Define Framework and Scope**
      * Mantra: More time in planning, less time in implementation
      * Put everything in the wall 
        * Cut 20% of the content 
        * Bucket the content into the Category
          * Discovery
          * Segment
          * Launch
        * How the brain work
          * 1,2,3, many
          * If you have more than 7 step, you must chunk down so that people can implement 
          * The less is better
        * Ryan decide to do with 3x3 framework

  * 3. Content Approach

        * Example Deep Dive Why
      * 2. Auditory / Musical - "hear"
        * Audio Download
      * 3. Verbal / Linguistic - "words"

      * 4. Physical / Kinesthetic - "do"
        * Go to the group
